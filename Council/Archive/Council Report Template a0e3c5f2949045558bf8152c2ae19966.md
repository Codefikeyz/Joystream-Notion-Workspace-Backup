# **Council Term Report Template**

*Comment: The headline of a document  should be formatted as "Council Report - Term #1", where #1 is replaced with the relevant Council term number.*

## **1. Summary**

**Status at the start of the term**

(1) pre-term status, goals and (unaddressed) problems.

(2) Compilation of candidate programs.

**Status at the end of the term**

(1) Comprehensive report on the financial information

(2) Overview of all tasks including which tasks have been delayed and the reasons for the delays, as well as revised ETA.

**Conclusion**

Expectations and outcomes for next term(s) and adaptation of the mid-term roadmap (may contain items that are only measurable after term ended). Basically a list of “balls in the air” for future CM to keep an eye and react to when necessary.
