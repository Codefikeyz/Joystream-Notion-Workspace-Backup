## 3**. Term Highlights**

- This term implemented the [chief council experiment](https://pioneerapp.xyz/#/forum/thread/972), where all three winning candidates in the election adhered to the agreed-upon structure. In this scheme, the candidate with the most votes or the [Chief CM](https://pioneerapp.xyz/#/proposals/preview/980) decided on proposals, while the other two council members aligned their votes accordingly.
- The [Argo Bridge](https://bridge.joystream.org/) was fully launched.
- A fireside chat was held on X Space (formerly Twitter Spaces).
- Working with the HR Lead, the council successfully onboarded our first [Infrastructure Lead](https://pioneerapp.xyz/#/proposals/preview/1005) and a Content Manager for the Marketing Working Group.
- The council passed the first round of [approval](https://pioneerapp.xyz/#/proposals/preview/1006) to reduce council salaries by half.
- The council [appointed](https://pioneerapp.xyz/#/proposals/preview/982) a DAO member as an official steward of joystream.org and a [new Discord admin](https://pioneerapp.xyz/#/proposals/preview/983).
- Discussions were initiated regarding [Q4 OKRs](https://pioneerapp.xyz/#/forum/thread/979) and the [Roadmap Update for Q4](https://pioneerapp.xyz/#/forum/thread/984).
- Four leadership positions were opened, including a new opening for an [Apps Lead](https://pioneerapp.xyz/#/working-groups/openings/apps-13). Two of these positions were filled: the Infrastructure Lead and Content Manager roles.
- At the end of the term, the pending YPP Rewards payments were settled, and the YPP syncing issue was resolved by JSG.
- Implementing the [EMA30 cap at 0.015](https://pioneerapp.xyz/#/proposals/preview/965) was also done this term, resulting in a significant decrease in DAO-wide spending and the lowest token issuance since Term 31.

**Challenges**

- The absence of leads in two working groups impacted operations:
    1. The lack of a CWG lead and the inability to hire one affected YPP Operations, particularly the planned YPP review and modifications.
    2. The vacancy in the DWG Lead position required significant time and effort from the council to find replacements.
- The sudden resignation announcement of the SWG lead at the beginning of the term forced the council to refocus on finding an Infrastructure Lead instead of managing both working groups (SWG+DWG).
- The overall market conditions made it difficult for the MWG to execute certain activities related to the Argo Bridge Launch and to fill roles within the DAO. Additionally, the sentiment from the community in the communication channels was not particularly positive.
- Issues surrounding YPP caused discontent among creators. While two major issues were resolved, one remains and is currently being addressed by the Infrastructure Lead and JSG.
