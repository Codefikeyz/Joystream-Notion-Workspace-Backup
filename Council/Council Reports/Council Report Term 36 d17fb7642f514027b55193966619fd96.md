This term marked the first time no plan was approved by Council due to differing opinions among the CMs on what should be included. We had two proposals for each plan, but both were rejected. The V2 proposal received 2 votes, while V1 received 1 vote. In the report, we'll primarily refer to V2 since it garnered more support.
