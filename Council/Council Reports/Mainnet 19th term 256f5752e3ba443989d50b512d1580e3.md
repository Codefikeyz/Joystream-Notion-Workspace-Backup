- Fill stablecoin treasury
- Finalize DAO budgeting/salary system
- SWG/DWG budget/salary and worker requirement
    - To date there is no approved compensation scheme for either WG which is leading to confusion
- MWG should prioritize exchange listing and ambassador program
- Test lead scoring system from HR
- Shut down Apps WG
    - During the last term there was one person on Discord who showed interest in signing up for the Apps WG to run a gateway/app but it is unclear how this progressed.
- Come up with a document or a guide for Safety Work Policies and/or perhaps work on JoystreamDAO Guidebook
- Review and implement Q4 OKRs; assess Q3 OKRs
- Ensure the timely delivery of the Weekly Summary
