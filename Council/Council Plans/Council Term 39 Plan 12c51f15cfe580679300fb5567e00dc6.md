## **2. Council Priorities and Working Group Tasks**

- **YPP**: Coordinate with Jsgenesis, builders, curators and support team to make sure program is up and running; investigate the persisting YPP issues; restore opted out channels and resume payouts.
- **CDN Optimization**: Work with the infrastructure lead on optimizing the content delivery costs; setup archive servers; reduce replication factor to 3; investigate & delete large trash files from Joystream library.
- **BASE ecosystem:** Continue communication with Aerodrome finance team regarding potential listing on Aero DEX; Increase DEX liquidity/adjust current LP parameters; engage with TrustWallet team to get JoystreamERC20 whitelisted on their wallet; execute checks for bridge signers availability.
- **Operational Tasks**: Process regular proposals & hiring; maintain council/working groups communication; facilitate USDT server compensation for CDN workers; handle routine governance matters.
- **Apps**: Support initiatives to improve Atlas documentation and tutorials for app launching; Develop strategies to attract potential builders to create applications on top of the protocol.

*Note: The above items will be prioritized for this term, if there will be room for more, the council may reconsider other tasks.*
