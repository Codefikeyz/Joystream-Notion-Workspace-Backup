# How to create a Funding proposal: Lead Bonuses

- select the add new proposal button and choose **Funding Request**

![Untitled](How%20to%20create%20a%20Funding%20proposal%20Lead%20Bonuses/Untitled.png)

- **Select account for Staking.** You must stake **25,000 tJOY** to create this proposal
- Specify the **Proposal title - Rhodes #24 (*The number of the term*) Lead Bonuses For (*Lead’s name*)**
- In the **Rationale** section, specify the following information:               ****

Rhodes #**(*The number of the term*)** Calculated Lead Bonuses approved - specify the active link to the Forum’s threat in which the calculated bonuses are posted

(***Amount of Lead bonuses***) k tJOY will be sent to the ROOT account of the (***Specify WG***) WG leader

(***Amount of Lead bonuses in* k tJOY**) k tJOY = (***Amount of Lead bonuses in JOY***) JOY on the last course in Retool ([https://joystream.retool.com/embedded/public/9478bcc0-f6d6-4f74-bd0a-0637535e75b1](https://joystream.retool.com/embedded/public/9478bcc0-f6d6-4f74-bd0a-0637535e75b1))

- **Specific parameters**

Specify the number of Lead bonuses and specify the Leader’s Root account
