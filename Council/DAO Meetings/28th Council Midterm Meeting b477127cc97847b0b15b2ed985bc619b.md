## Meeting Note

## **Council Tasks**

- **Publish Vision for the DAO Structure:** A series of smaller proposals are underway, including the consolidation of Admin working groups into HR. A detailed signal proposal will be published this week, with further discussions ongoing.
- **Create Plan for Resources Reallocation within DAO:** Ongoing discussions aim to reduce spending on infrastructure and administration. Initiatives include exploring reductions in HR worker count post-consolidation.
- **Investigate CWG Rewards:** Conversations are ongoing to explore potential enhancements.
- **Long-term Approach to Liquidity and Market Making:** Discussions continue with no visible progress at present.
- **Finalize Q1’24 OKRs and Roadmap v2:** Roadmap v2 has been finalized and published on the Joystream website. Finalization of OKRs, pending marketing-related aspects, will be addressed in an upcoming call this week.

## Working Group Update

**Builders WG:**

- Progress continues on SWG/DWG support with tooling and infrastructure work.
- Discussions are ongoing to prioritize development in Argus and colossus nodes, with a focus on reducing replication rates.
- The validator module is nearing completion pending resolution of QA points.
- Plans to create a Nara runtime upgrade proposal and continue work on WalletConnect are underway.
- Progress is reported on Luxor upgrade, council treasury burn proposal, and strategic planning for BWG personnel.

**Distribution WG:**

- Investigations into LA node status and cost optimization efforts are ongoing.
- A proposal to introduce X-pre storage to the backend is being explored.

**Marketing WG:**

- Efforts to enhance the capacity of the working group include hiring additional workers, with the first opening for a content manager.
- Progress continues on campaign creation for CRT launch awareness, with plans to finalize video creation.
- Discussions on market-making tools and coordination with the council on marketing-related OKRs for Q1 2024 are pending.
- Ongoing efforts include Google AdWords campaigns, content creation, ambassador program initiation, and outreach to web3 projects and creators.
- Support for product launches and events includes Joystream token listing on additional exchanges and research into smaller exchanges.
- Efforts to engage with top Validators and organize posts for KOLs have made progress.

**Human Resources WG:**

- HR tasks are progressing smoothly, including scheduled fireside chats and ongoing surveys.
- Adoption of Google Calendar for Joystream events is underway.

**Content WG:**

- Ongoing tasks include curating videos and promoting NFTs across various channels.
- Coordination with Marketing WG for Twitter posting and updates to weights channel are in progress.
- Initiatives such as YPP Reports and forum moderation are underway.

**Forum WG:**

- Forum moderation, archiving, and transfer of announcements are being executed effectively.

**Membership WG:**

- All tasks are progressing according to plan.
