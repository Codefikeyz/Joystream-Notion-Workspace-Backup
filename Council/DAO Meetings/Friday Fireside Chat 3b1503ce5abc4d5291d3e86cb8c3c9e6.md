## Twitter Space Fireside Chat

1st September 2023

**Session Overview:**

**Leet_Joy: Ambassadorship Program**

- **Introduction to Ambassadorship Program:** Leet_Joy introduced the Ambassadorship program, shedding light on its purpose and significance within Joystream.
- **Program Design:** The design of the Ambassadorship program was discussed, including its structure and organization.
- **Scope of Participation:** The scope of participation in the program was explored, specifying who can become an ambassador.
- **Category:** Different categories within the Ambassadorship program were outlined, providing a comprehensive understanding of the various roles.
- **Payment Reward Options:** Reward options for ambassadors, detailing how they can be compensated for their contributions.
- **Recognition:** Recognition, both within the community and beyond, was highlighted as an essential component of the Ambassadorship program.

**Bedeho: YPP and Gleev**

- **Overview of the Gleev App and its Vision:** Bedeho provided an extensive overview of the Gleev App, emphasizing its vision and mission.
- **An Introduction to YPP:** The YouTube Partner Program was introduced, shedding light on its purpose and relevance.
- **Conditions for Participation in YPP:** Bedeho discussed the conditions and requirements for content creators to participate in YPP.
- **Purpose of Crypto-Only Content:** An explanation was given regarding the purpose of crypto-only content and its role in the Joystream ecosystem.
- **New Access to Non-Crypto Content in YPP:** Information was shared about the opening of YPP to non-crypto content creators, broadening its reach.
- **Prominent Features of Crypto Contents on Homepage:**  Crypto based contents are still going to dominate the gleev homepage in acceptance of non-crypto content
- **More Tiers Coming Through with Bigger Incentives:** Future developments in YPP, including additional tiers with increased incentives, were discussed.
- **Gleev: Open Source for All:** The open-source nature of Gleev and its accessibility to the wider community were emphasized.
- **Requirements to Build an App on Joystream:** The requirements for building an application on the Joystream platform were discussed.
- **The Long-Term Goal of Joystream:** Bedeho shared insights into the long-term goals and aspirations of Joystream as a project.

**OTC Deal - Leet_Joy**

- **Why Do We Need Stablecoin?:** The necessity of stablecoins within the Joystream ecosystem was explored, addressing its importance.
- **Paying for Infrastructure with Stable Coins:** The use of stablecoins for funding infrastructure and operational expenses was discussed.
- **Multisig Wallet Custodians:** The concept of multisig wallet custodians was introduced, explaining their role in securing funds.
- **Spending Threshold and Council Approval Quorum:** Details were provided about spending thresholds and the quorum required for council approval.
- **Funding Limit:** The possible limitation of funds being sent to this multisig wallet was discussed
- **Replacing Custodians:** Procedures for replacing custodians and ensuring the security of multisig wallets were highlighted.
- **Nomination of Signers: currently** ongoing in the [forum thread here](https://pioneerapp.xyz/#/forum/thread/568)

**OTC Deal - 0x2bc**

- **Overview of the Multisig Wallet Policy:** The policy outlined for the multisig wallets was discussed, ensuring transparency and accountability.
- **Which Stablecoin Token to Adopt:** Deliberations took place regarding the choice of stablecoin tokens to adopt within for stablecoin treasury.
- **How to Procure Stablecoin Tokens:** Strategies for procuring stablecoin tokens were discussed, considering market dynamics.
- **Avoiding Long-Term Market Volatility:** mitigating long-term market volatility and safeguarding the $JOY token price as a reason for stablecoin treasury.
- **Initiating OTC Transactions:** How this OTC transaction can be initiated to maintain token price stability.
- **Legalities of OTC Transactions:** Complex legal aspects of OTC transactions within a DAO like Joystream were acknowledged and discussed.

**Creator Tokens - Bedeho**

- **Issuance and Trading of Creator Tokens:** The process of issuing and trading creator tokens was elucidated, highlighting their significance.
- **An Enabler for Content Creators:** Creator tokens were discussed as an enabler for content creators within the Joystream ecosystem.
- **Financialization as the Mission:** Bedeho emphasized the mission of financialization enabled by creator tokens.
- **Soon Coming Release of V1:** Anticipation for the upcoming release of V1, featuring exciting new features.

**Question: Does Joystream Have a Hardware Wallet?**

- The absence of hardware wallet support for Joystream was confirmed.
- The use of a Parity Signer, a mobile application for offline mobile devices, through QR code-signed transactions, which is currently working on substrate-based chains, discovered by Klaudiusz was mentioned.

**Hardware Wallets - Tomato**

- **Need for Custom Coded Integration with Ledger:** The need for a fully custom coded and audited integration with Ledger for operational compatibility makes it difficult for adoption
- **Storage Capacity Limitations:** Limitations related to storage capacity on hardware wallets and their potential impact on Joystream's custom functionalities.
- **Exploring Hardware Wallets for Substrate-Based Chains:** progressive search for hardware wallets that can support Joystream, a substrate-based chain.
- **Considering Ethereum Bridging:** Due to the high requirements of Ledger operability, the option of waiting for Ethereum bridging was considered as an alternative.

**End of Session.**
