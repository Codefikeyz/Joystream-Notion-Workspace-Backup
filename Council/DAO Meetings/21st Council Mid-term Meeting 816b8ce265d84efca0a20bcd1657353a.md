## Meeting Note

---

**Agenda:**

**1. DAO Workers Assessment:**

- The ongoing survey indicates good compliance among DAO workers.
- We've gathered feedback from over 50% of workers so far.
- Our objective is to gather more feedback to ensure robust data analysis.

**2. Working Group Updates:**

**Storage Working Group:**

- Discussion on the need for a dedicated developer to monitor and maintain the storage server codebase.
- Details on responsibilities and compensation for this role will be provided.

**Marketing Working Group:**

- Increased video review for "gleev."
- Monitoring YPP landing page visits.
- Completed YPP improvement research and submitted the findings to Jsgenesis.
- Suggestion made to Jsgenesis to introduce two levels of referral rewards.
- Upcoming publication of a video explainer about why JOY doesn't have a maximum supply, created by @bythekingmaker.

**Forum Working Group:**

- Moderation of Forum
- Archiving old pre-proposal discussions
- Follow up on Forum discussions action item

**Distribution Working Group:**

- Successful migration of nodes.
- Hired three operators.

**Membership Working Group:**

- Significant increase in daily sign-ups, exceeding the minimum requirement.
- Challenges due to high demand and a focus on monitoring user authenticity.
- Discussion about the need for additional funds for the Membership Working Group, which are currently being burnt instead of utilized or exchanged.

**Human Resources Working Group:**

- Conducted surveys for both DAO workers and content creators.
- Preparations for the weekly gleev/YPP call to support content creators.
- Completed the weekly report, awaiting council review.
- Distributed a survey to DAO workers to analyze working conditions.
- Reviewed the Leeds scoring system.

1. **Atlas Playback Issues and Solutions:**
- Discussed user reports of playback issues on Atlas.
- Uncertainty remains about whether users experienced storage node timeouts or other issues causing video loading delays.
- Agreed to collaborate on addressing the issue, including examining upload times for images and nodes.

1. **Including USD Valuation in Reports and Plans:**
- Council members are encouraged to review their plans and include USD valuations for better financial transparency.

**End of Session**
