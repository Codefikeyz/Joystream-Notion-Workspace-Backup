### **Meeting Notes:**

**1. Lead Report and Planning for Better Flow:**

- Chaos led a discussion on reformatting the DAO's report and plan submission process. The primary goal is to enhance efficiency and minimize time wastage. This initiative aims to streamline information gathering and presentation to ensure that reports serve their intended purpose effectively.

**2. Q3 OKR Analysis by Tomato:**

- Tomato presented an in-depth analysis of the Q3 Objectives and Key Results (OKRs). This comprehensive review provides valuable insights into the achievements and areas where improvements are needed. Visit the Q3 OKR review link for detailed information: [Pioneer](https://pioneerapp.xyz/#/forum/thread/577)

**3. Q4 OKR Planning Discussion:**

- This focused on outlining the objectives for the upcoming Q4. Key points addressed included:
    - A thorough examination of the Gateway Operation Objective.
    - Deliberation on the operation of the Apps Working Group (with Lead responsible for developing the plan).
    - Addressing the issue of low profitability in the previous Q3, with insights and inputs from various members:
        - Tomato emphasized the need for enhanced data tracking.
        - Chaos proposed strategies to control inflation.
        - Discussion on a more effective system than viewing sell pressure negatively, considering that workers are exchanging tokens they earned by Yasir.
        - Question of establishing clear upper and lower bounds for inflation.
        - It was agreed that the council would work on developing a suitable methodology for addressing these concerns.
- For further details, visit the Q4 discussion link: [Pioneer App](https://pioneerapp.xyz/#/forum/thread/587).

**4. DAO Spending Discussion:**

- A crucial discussion revolved around whether to increase DAO spending. Key points covered included:
    - Exploration of the possibility of setting limits on the council budget.
    - In-depth conversation about the rate and amount of salary payments to ensure they align with the DAO's financial health.
    - Reference to supporting documents for a comprehensive understanding:
        - A brief rundown of working group spending and activities: [Google Docs](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1I7DIUvn3q8XTVcQ7GO5txrzBjYyC2kFRPki8VO7HZnw/edit).
        - A view of working group spending chart: [Dapp Looker](https://dapplooker.com/dashboard/joystream-working-group-spending-dashboard-356).
        - Consideration of a proposition from SWG regarding rewards: [GitHub](https://github.com/yasiryagi/community-repo/blob/master/working-groups/storage-group/leader/Proposals/Salary_cal.md#comparison-with-the-current-status).

**5. DAO Welfare Discussion:**

- A comprehensive discussion was held on DAO welfare, including:
    - Examination of the current compensation structure.
    - An overview of the council budget.
    - Plans for creating a detailed spreadsheet to monitor DAO spending over an extended timeframe while factoring in inflation and other variables.
    - Links to reference materials for more info:
        - DAO welfare discussion link: [Pioneer App](https://pioneerapp.xyz/#/forum/thread/586).
        - Access to the DAO salary assessment: [Notion](https://www.notion.so/DAO-Salary-Assesment-5b1ded42fa0340048737fb1f7934870b?pvs=21).

**6. Discussion of 360 Review System:**

- The meeting included an exploration of a 360-degree review system, proposed by Yasir where workers assess leads and council members. Key points addressed included:
    - The development of a specific grading scheme and the questions that individuals will be asked to grade against.
    - A commitment to continue the discussion to refine and implement this valuable feedback mechanism.

**7. Runtime Upgrade:**

- Detailed discussions regarding the runtime upgrade, including:
    - A thorough examination of issues related to the runtime upgrade.
    - Decision-making processes for the upgrade plan, with a strong emphasis on Council involvement.
    - Coordination with the JSG for the `NARA`runtime upgrade.
    - The DAO's role in taking ownership of parameters for change and developing a comprehensive scope of change, along with considerations of timeframe and other factors.

**8. Discussion on Council Secretary Role:**

- Deliberation on the need for a Council Secretary position, including:
    - Alignment of responsibilities with the current state and needs of the DAO.
    - Tomato to review the Council Secretary role.
