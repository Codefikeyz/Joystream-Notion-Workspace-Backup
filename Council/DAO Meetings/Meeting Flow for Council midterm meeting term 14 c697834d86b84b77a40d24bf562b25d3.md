**Meeting Flow:**

I. Welcome and Introduction by Council Secretary (2 mins)

- Begin the meeting by warmly welcoming all participants.
- Provide a concise overview of the meeting's agenda.
- Present a brief summary of the meeting flow.

II. Overview of Working Group Plan by Council (10 mins)

- Council members present an overview of the plans for each Working Group.
- Emphasize any significant challenges or specific issues requiring attention.

III. Progress Evaluation and Issue Resolution: moderated by Council Secretary (2 mins each)

- Allow each Working Group to provide a concise progress update on their ongoing activities.
- Highlight any current issues that require attention or resolution.
- The order of updates:
    - HR WG
    - MWG
    - DWG
    - SWG
    - BWG
    - APPs WG
    - FWG
    - CWG
- Update on Council Operations

IV. Open Discussion by Council: Shaping the Future of the DAO (15 mins)

- Facilitate an open discussion led by the Council to brainstorm and share ideas on shaping the future of the DAO.
- Capture key ideas and suggestions for further discussion and potential implementation.

V. Wrap-up and Next Steps by Council (10 mins)

- Summarize the main points discussed during the meeting.
- Highlight key action items, decisions, or follow-up tasks that need to be addressed.
- Provide an opportunity for participants to ask questions or share any final thoughts.

VI. Meeting Conclusion (13:53 CET)

- Express gratitude to all participants for their active participation and valuable contributions.
- Officially conclude the meeting.

Note: The times provided in the agenda are estimates and may vary slightly during the actual meeting.

**Rules and Guidelines:**

- To maintain efficiency, it is recommended to follow the schedule of events. The meeting host may interrupt speakers who have consumed all the allotted time, giving them up to 1 additional minute.
- Allotted periods for questions and discussion should be utilized, with attendees advised not to interrupt speakers unless urgent.
- Attendees should join the voice channel at least a minute early to ensure a timely start.
