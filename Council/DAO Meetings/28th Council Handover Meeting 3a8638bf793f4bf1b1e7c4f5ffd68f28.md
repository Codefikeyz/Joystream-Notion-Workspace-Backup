## Meeting Note

**27th Term Council Highlights:**

**Roadmap v2 for Joystream Website:**

- Council and core community contributors discussed and agreed upon Roadmap v2 for the Joystream website.
- The update will be implemented on the website shortly.

**Nara Runtime Upgrade:**

- The delivery timeframe for the upcoming Nara runtime upgrade was solidified.
- The upgrade is scheduled to be executed within approximately one month.

**User Signups via YPP:**

- A notable increase in new user signups via YPP was observed towards the end of the term.

**Liquidity Addition to Joystream Markets:**

- The council decided to inject $10k USDT of liquidity into Joystream markets across various exchanges for the first time.
- A liquidity provision worker was assigned to oversee this initiative.

**Restructuring and Resource Allocation Discussions:**

- Extensive discussions commenced regarding potential restructuring, consolidation, and resource allocation adjustments for the DAO.
- Enactment of these discussions by a future council may contribute to saving DAO funds and optimizing the organization.

**DAO’s Budget Increment Parameter:**

- The DAO's budget increment parameter was deliberated, and a proposal was made to adjust it for the first time since the project was listed on an exchange.

**Working Group Updates:**

*Builders WG:*

- Subsquid version of Argus and Colossus merged, currently under testing.
- Fixes made to Argus and Colossus.
- Changes to the distribution ping tool were developed for improved integration of storage servers, along with Discord alerts.
- QA plan prepared for the validator module.
- Significant progress on Nara, tesnet upgrade scheduled for tomorrow.
- Long-term planning initiated for future runtime upgrades.
- Ongoing work on wallet-connect implementation, with design completed, library in progress, and initial integration with Atlas underway.
- Adjustments made to the Discord role bot.
- Servers developed to facilitate external tools in fetching objects from the CDN without GraphQL or processing.
- Video fees estimation added to Joyutils.

*Marketing WG:*

- Expansion to 700 new channels.
- Accumulation of $400k JOY through market-making tools.
- Curation work for a Google AdWord campaign.
- Outreach to creators and crypto projects.
- Ambassador preparation and new openings creation.
- Active social media posting.
- Outreach to potential validators.
- Research on exchanges and bridges.

*Forum WG:*

- Creation of 39 new threads, totaling 825, with 36 from founding members and 3 from non-founding members.
- 170 new posts, reaching a total of 6047.
- Introduction of new sub-categories and follow-up on stale forum posts.

*Content WG:*

- Increased workload for curators.
- Addition of one curator to the YPP team.
- Verification of 170 new channels today, with approximately 450 channels in the queue.

*Membership WG:*

- Significant increase in new signups toward the end of the last term.
- Preparation for the consolidation of admin working groups.

*Storage WG:*

- Termination of one worker.
- Consolidation of two workers into one.

*HR WG:*

- Implementation of approved Bounty Process v1 completed.
- Weekly report published on time, lacking YPP payout data.
- Efficient moderation carried out with room for improvement.
- Recruitment of a community moderator with successful onboarding.
- Regular updates to EMA30 for DAO budgeting, reflecting a decline due to a decrease in JOY price.
- Community events scheduled for the upcoming term.
- Ongoing support and improvement in responses to FAQs.
- Council election facilitation with announcement and statistics update.
- Continuous improvement in information dissemination through daily announcements and updates.

*Distribution WG:*

- Addition of a node in India.
- Resolution of CDN issues.
- Resolution of issues with the LA node.
- Removal of the New York node.
- Investigation into the need for cloudworkers.
- Performance evaluation of Joystream CDN and search dashboard.

**Recommendations:**

- Work on reducing SWG spending by implementing a replication factor of two.
- Discuss and vote on the "Budget Increment Proposal" within five days of being elected, as it will expire after that time period.
- Discuss consolidation decisions, such as pausing the Apps WG and possibly merging Forum + Membership WGs into HR.
- Conduct a review and solicit feedback on the EMA-30 pricing tool.
- Review the presentation of validator rewards in Council plans and reports.
- Follow up on the impact of the recently enacted liquidity injection into Joystream markets.
- Assess all DAO working groups, with particular attention to the HR WG.
- Discuss DAO resource allocation.
- Evaluate the possibility of delaying the Nara runtime upgrade to consider a validator reward curve adjustment.
- Deliberate on the pre-proposal regarding the Builder WG lead's salary.
- Consider the pre-proposal regarding "Tomato Compensation Discussion."
- Explore the assignment of trusted community members for OTC deal negotiation due to discomfort in public negotiations.

**Discussion Points:**

- Automate YPP channel verification to save costs.
- Enhance online presence for improved visibility.

**End of Session**
