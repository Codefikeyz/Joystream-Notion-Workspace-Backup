**Agenda:**

**I. HR Working Group Updates**

Mariia, provided a comprehensive update on the group's progress. Tasks and responsibilities have been successfully assigned to each team member, ensuring clear accountability. Furthermore, Mariia shared that a detailed review of the composition of the previous working group has been conducted, allowing for necessary adjustments and improvements. Notably, a pre-proposal thread has been published to gather input and suggestions regarding an increase in the DAO quiz rewards. In addition, efforts are underway to recruit a skilled content writer for the Marketing Working Group.

**II. Marketing Working Group Updates**

Leet_Joy, delivered an insightful update on the group's activities. The focus of the team is to enhance the value perception of Joystream within the external community. To achieve this, MWG is developing innovative strategies that aim to maximise marketing impact while optimising costs. Notably, a key initiative involves incentivizing YPP users to generate and promote high-quality content on their respective YouTube channels. Leet_Joy also highlighted that the Marketing Working Group has proactively engaged with several exchanges to explore potential zero-fee listing opportunities, leveraging partnerships for increased visibility.

**III. Distribution Working Group Updates**

Mokhtar, the newly appointed lead of the Distribution Working Group, shared a comprehensive overview of the group's progress. With a keen focus on aligning their objectives with the broader council goals, the DWGL has meticulously planned and budgeted their activities. Mokhtar has also prioritised familiarising himself with the tools and processes set up by the previous Lead. Notably, the new lead is actively working on developing an approach that facilitates smoother transitions for future operational needs, ensuring seamless continuity.

**IV. Storage Working Group Update**

Yassir, provided a concise update on their recent activities. While there have been no major developments within the group, Yassir highlighted an ongoing dialogue with Subsquid regarding potential experimental utilisation of their software. This initiative demonstrates the Storage Working Group's proactive approach to exploring new opportunities and evaluating potential collaborations.

**Council Status Update**

- Marketing: The council continues to focus on improving the external community's perception of Joystream. Leet_Joy expressed the council's commitment to delivering visible progress before the end of 14th term.
- Network Security: In the interest of bolstering network security, the council has successfully increased the number of validators in the active set to 40. Additionally, efforts are being made to ensure the seamless operation of storage and distribution workers.
- Sustainability: The council proudly announced that sustainability has been achieved across all working group operations, indicating the successful alignment of resources and objectives.
- Long-Term Planning: The council is actively engaged in defining roadmap tasks for the upcoming quarter, diligently working toward the achievement of Q3 OKRs.

**Working Group Plans Template**

During the discussion, Mokhtar inquired about the availability of a standardised working group plans template. Leet_Joy promptly confirmed the existence of an approved template, ensuring consistent documentation of group plans. 0x2bc emphasised the importance of incorporating measurable objectives within the template, enabling effective progress tracking and evaluation.

**Hiring Plan**

DWGL and SWGL confirmed that there are currently no immediate plans for hiring additional workers in the distribution and storage groups. However, if necessary, future hiring decisions will be based on performance considerations and strategic expansion plans. Notably, the DWGL may consider filling an opening to establish a distribution node in a region where demand necessitates such an expansion.

**Relationship with Subsquid**

A suggestion was made for a council member to take the initiative in fostering and advancing the partnership with Subsquid. The council acknowledged the importance of proactive engagement and representation to leverage mutual benefits effectively.

**Leadership Transition**

Mokhtar highlighted the possibility of requiring a break due to health reasons and initiated a discussion regarding a potential leadership transition. The council engaged in productive dialogue, considering suitable candidates who could assume the lead position in the event of Mokhtar's absence
