## Meeting Note

**Term Highlights:**

- The council has approved the first constitutionality for the Luxor Runtime Upgrade proposal.
- Bridging to Sygma was cancelled in favor of building our own bridge, named Argo.
- CRT launch was postponed due to bugs, which have since been resolved.
- Collaborative efforts between CWG and JSG led to several improvements in Creator Token UX.
- A Fireside Chat on X was held with council members and leads.
- First two DAO-wide objectives for Q2 OKRs 2024 were agreed upon, and a Roadmap meeting took place.
- Final design for the Joystream branded Tangem wallet has been completed this term.
- There was a notable increase in uptime for DWG nodes following the Subsquid migration.
- Integration with StealthEx, similar to Changenow, has been accomplished.
- Council and work groups have adopted the Kanban Board for efficient task management.
- The first phase of the Ambassador Program concluded with 15 ambassadors onboarded.
- Various tasks pertaining to Luxor upgrade proposal, CRT atlas release, OKRs, Joystream bridge, and treasury stream were addressed.

**Working Group Status:CWG:**

- Focus on launching CRTs with stability achieved in the development environment.
- Q&A sessions conducted, bugs identified and fixed.
- Ambassadors and HR are testing the environment.
- Ongoing discussions on onboarding videos and YPP takeover from JSG.
- Progress on Creator Engagement Program, including dashboard design and landing page draft.

**MWG:**

- Established Kanban board for streamlined operations.
- Ambassador program applications closed with over 20 onboarded, 15 active.
- Engaging in social media management and blog posting.
- Progress made on digital marketing campaigns and CRT launch planning.
- Integration with StealthX completed.

**SWG:**

- Experienced turnover with new additions and bug fixes.
- Progress on recruiting a clustering architect.
- Initiatives include Luxor upgrade and Argo bridge development.
- Support provided for various atlas issues and CRT analytics.
- Maintenance and improvements to query nodes ongoing.

**BWG:**

- Completed Luxor upgrade development and Q&A.
- Initiated work on Argo bridge and Ethereum contract updates.
- Support provided for atlas issues and CRT analytics.
- Ongoing development of Joystream apps library and maintenance tasks.

**HRWG:**

- Shortlisted candidates for CEPH architect position drafting statements of work.
- DAO workers survey ongoing.
- Validators verification in progress.
- Daily announcements and election stats updates conducted.
- Discord moderation maintained for a safe environment.
- Meeting notes now being taken regularly.

**Recommendations for the Next Council:**

- Review and pass a proposal for Q2 OKRs within the 1st week of the term.
- Review lead salaries and tasks alignment.
- Consider implementing daily updates for all DAO workers.
- Provide necessary support to CWG for CRT Release and YPP payments.
- Review/assess Q1 2024 OKRs.
- Consider hiring a marketing agency to promote creator tokens outside the web3 community.

**End of Session.**
