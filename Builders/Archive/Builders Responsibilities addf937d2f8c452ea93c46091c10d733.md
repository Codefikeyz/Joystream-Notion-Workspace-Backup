# Builders Responsibilities

## All Workers

Keeps an eye on assigned tickets and reviews for requested changes / QA remarks until it gets merged: [`https://github.com/Joystream/pioneer/issues?q=assignee%](https://github.com/Joystream/pioneer/issues?q=assignee%3Agyroflaw)[YOUR_HANDLE]`

After merge reports spent hours per PR to Deputy.

Self-assigns via message on GH or checks in at [**#builders**](https://discord.com/channels/811216481340751934/812344874099277824) when idle.

## Training

1. Assists with membership creation, applying to an opening and pioneer setup.
2. Encourages and motivates the team to work hard to meet high Council’s and JSG expectations
3. Moderates the `#builders` channel.

#######

## Deputy

1. Reports term plan on the forum.
2. Maintains Builders [Notion space](../../Builders%20ffb1c9d1d1094fc4a6f04eb47677673d.md).
3. Ensures workers are paid according to their performance
    1. Tracks workers performance, collect hours per task from all workers. ⇒ Notion
    2. Suggests new worker rewards based on reported hours and SP ⇒ Notion
    3. Finalizes rewards together with Lead ⇒ Notion
4. Prepares weekly reports in a format approved by Council 
    
    

## Lead

1. Lead’s main responsibility is to maximize [`Builders Score`](https://joystream.gitbook.io/testnet-workspace/testnet/council-period-scoring/builders-score).
2. #### Keeps contact to JSG PO for clarifications (GH, discord, ad-hoc zoom call format as agreed).####
3. Ensures each worker has at least one item to work on.
4. Creates new tasks in [Project 56](https://github.com/orgs/Joystream/projects/56), a.k.a Community backlog.
5. Reviews [Project 55](https://github.com/orgs/Joystream/projects/55) backlog, assigns labels to tickets in accordance to the approved process.
6. Lead may pick up approved tasks from either [Project 55](https://github.com/orgs/Joystream/projects/55) or [Project 56](https://github.com/orgs/Joystream/projects/56). In such a case, Lead reports hours as normal worker to the Deputy
7. On a monthly basis, fires non-responsive workers who did not produce valuable deliverables. 
8. In conjunction with Council adjusts hourly rate. 
9. ####Makes sure there are always active Openings####
