### 0. Status of the Squad and Squad workers in JSG setup

Any company leverages either in-house workforce, or external contractors. In certain cases the freelance workforce is also used, and even unpaid internships. Every working model entails its own legal status and its own advantages/disadvantages. 

An open question to JSG in the light of the above is how the Builders Squad is viewed and what legal framework (if any at all) is expected to govern the working relations between the Builders Squad and JSG. 

Involving the Builders Squad into JSG flagship products ([Pioneer](https://dao.joystream.org) and [Atlas](http://play.joystream.org/)) development lifecycle may be seen as an [extended team](https://developmentaid.it/extended-teams-model-how-does-it-work/) model that is quite commonplace nowadays. However, from a perspective of a company willing to use ETM to meet its delivery targets, if must secure the competitive compensation and legal framework for extension workers, neither of which follows from JSG initial view. 

**One thing that seems to be the shared understanding on all levels: Builders is the special and unique Squad, not like any other squad within Joystream community.** 

Unique problems call for creative solutions.

**Outcome. JSG is not in the position to sign any contracts with Builders.**
