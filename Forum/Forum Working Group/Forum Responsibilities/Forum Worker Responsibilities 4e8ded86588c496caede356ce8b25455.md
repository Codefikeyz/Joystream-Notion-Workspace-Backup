# Forum Worker Responsibilities

Forum Worker is responsible for overlooking the forum.

- monitors the Forum activity;
- takes over facilitation, review, and guidance of a discussion or a debate and its related interactions;
- uses a standard or guideline to ensure that all the content shared during a discussion or debate is appropriate and adheres to the Forum Moderation Rule rules;
- deletion of unnecessary, unapplicable or erroneous threads or posts;
- movement of wrongly categorized threads or posts;
- sticky threading necessary or important threads or posts.
