# Forum Lead Responsibilities

Forum lead is responsible overlooking the forum and forum workers

- monitors and moderates the Forum activity;
    - works on new categories;
    - movement of wrongly or not updated categorized threads or posts;
    - sticky threading necessary or important threads or posts;
    - deletion of unnecessary, unapplicable or erroneous threads or posts;
- arranges and updates notion page for Forum group;
- prepares necessary reports per council term;
    - creates group plans at the beginning of council term;
    - summarized forum summaries and group reports at the end of term;
- attends meetings and handover calls with council and other leads;
    - reports group plans and forum summaries;
    - brainstorm with council and other leads;
- manages and distributes extra budget via discretionary spending;
- helps managing stale pre-proposals;
    - reminds or follow ups authors and council;
    - write summary with open questions for stale threads;
- works with builder’s team regarding bugs and improvements related to forum;
    - documenting bugs or improvements and making github reports;
    - testing builder’s updates and giving necessary feedback;
- manages Forum workers and checks their work; (N/A at the moment)
    - assigns moderators to categories;
    - executes hiring and firing of forum workers;

Forum Deputy Lead assists Lead works in case the latter is not available
