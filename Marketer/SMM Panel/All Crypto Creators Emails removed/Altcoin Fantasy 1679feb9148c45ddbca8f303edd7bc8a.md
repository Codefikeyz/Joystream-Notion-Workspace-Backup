Description: About Altcoin Fantasy: 

Altcoin Fantasy is a fun and realistic app for paper trading and testing your skills at trading cryptocurrency. Learn how to trade cryptocurrencies (crypto) and Bitcoin in our FREE crypto trading simulator. All prices are in real time and from the live order book so you're paper trading crypto in real time. Compete against players from all around the world in our free game!

How Altcoin Fantasy works:

You start with a fantasy portfolio during each trading competition. You research different cryptocurrencies and tokens using crypto charts, crypto news and crypto descriptions and buy the coins you think will perform the best. At the end of the trading competition, the top players will win real crypto! Practice and hone your skills as a cryptocurrency trader without actually losing real money! All the fun and reward with none of the risk of trading real crypto.
