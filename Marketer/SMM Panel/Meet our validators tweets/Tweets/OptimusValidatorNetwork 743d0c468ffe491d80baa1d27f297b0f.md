OptimusValidatorNetwork was born at the beginning of 2021, when we started digging into the Crypto world and started to use our background and skills to support new projects. Since then we have supported over 20 projects. Now we have ~20 servers between instances and dedicated. Each of our mainnet node has a backup node and automated scripting to launch it if a fault in the main node is detected. We continuously improve automation and monitoring.
