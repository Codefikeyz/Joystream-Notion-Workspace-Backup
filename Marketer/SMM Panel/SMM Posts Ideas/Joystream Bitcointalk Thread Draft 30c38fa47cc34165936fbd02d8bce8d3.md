To highlight:

- Project was launched recently, this is a “new” project
    - Joystream is an open source platform, with an open blockchain at its core, powering multiple video streaming Apps at once. Videos uploaded via any app built on Joystream can reach consumers of other apps on the network, allowing creators to expand their audience with the single account without extra effort.
- Gleev etc
    - **Gleev**
    Gleev has its sole focus on the Web3 and Crypto content vertical, bringing together the like-minded creators and viewers around the shared passion of the present and the future of Crypto affairs
