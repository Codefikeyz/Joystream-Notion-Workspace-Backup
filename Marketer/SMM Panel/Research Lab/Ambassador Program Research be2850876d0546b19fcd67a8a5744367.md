### PRINT3R

PRINT3R is a gamified decentralized derivatives platform on Coinbase’s Ethereum scaling network, Base. Focusing on user-friendly interactions and shared revenue to promote user loyalty, the platform allows users to trade liquid crypto assets with up to 50x leverage, eliminating the complexities of KYC or registration.

### Tiered system

Bronze

- Monthly Prize Pool: A chance to share in their monthly prize pool, starting at $5,000 (with potential growth).
- Bronze Tier badge in discord

Silver

- Networking: Exclusive opportunities to connect with crypto loyal and fellow ambassadors.
- Internship & Job Opportunities: Showcase users prowess and there is potential to join their team
- PRINT3R merchandise: Exclusive Merchandise to showcase affiliation with PRINT3R
- Silver Tier badge in discord

Gold

- Exclusive Invitations: VIP access to their events, product launches, and more.
- The PRINT3R Twitter Affiliation Badge: Wear it as a badge of honor, signaling elite status in the PRINT3R community.
- Gold Tier badge in discord
- 1-1 Access to the core team
- Collaboration Opportunities: Allow ambassadors to collaborate on official PRINT3R projects or campaigns.

### Requirements

- All Are Welcome: No minimum followers or subscribers required. They believe in quality and authenticity over quantity.
- Platform Diversity: They embrace content creators from all countries & major platforms – whether tweeting in Chinese on Twitter, vlogging in Spanish on YouTube, streaming on Twitch, or sharing on Facebook.
- Twitter-Specific Requirement: For those using Twitter, a subscription to Twitter Blue (indicated by the blue badge) is a prerequisite for participation.

### AssetX

Decentralized platform AssetX offers a fusion of Real World Assets (RWAs), crypto DeFi investments, and early-stage venture capital opportunities.

### How to join

- Apply to become an AssetX Ambassador by filling out the form
- Promote AssetX within communities via your established social media presence. (NOTE: Do not “shill,” but rather inform.)
- Encourage users to join the AssetX Telegram group: https://t.me/AssetXOfficial
- Teach inexperienced users of BNB Chain how to use the network.
- Engage with AssetX news and posts across their social media pages, including Twitter, Medium, and Telegram.
- Be present and engage with each other and interact with users within the AssetX Telegram and other social channels.

### Requirements

- Basic Comprehension of Marketing: Understanding marketing strategies and applications.
- Gather Insights and Report Back: Ability to collect data, analyze, and provide feedback.
- Engaging Online Presence: Maintain a lively and interactive online presence.
- Professionalism: Uphold professional conduct in all interactions and promotions. Community
- Leadership: Ability to guide and lead your community effectively.
- Authentic Excitement: Genuine enthusiasm and excitement about the AssetX project. If you are confident about having the attributes, you are one step closer to becoming an ambassador!

### Rewards

- Direct one-to-one contact with the AssetX core team.
- AssetX's native $ASX tokens paid up to 10,000 $ASX (~ $1000 at TGE) over three months per ambassador.

### DMAIL

Powered by artificial intelligence, DMAIL Network is building a decentralized infrastructure that offers seamless, anonymous messaging and notifications spanning multiple blockchains and applications. In 2021, the platform secured $10 million in a Seed round.

### Requirements

- Having a passion of Web 3.0, our vision and mission.
- Very active and engaging on Discord, Telegram, and Twitter.
- Experience of community engagement on social media & meetups.
- Experience of working with Dfinity ecosystem
- Content creation skills.

### Rewards

- Competitive salary and rewards incentives.
- Commission from the Dmail presale in your area.
- Invitations to our events as a privileged guest organizer.
- Access to Dmail Network alums.
- You may visit any of our offices at any time.
- Your feedback has a chance to be written to the Dmail Roadmap.
- Limited edition swags.

### Evaluation

- They will observe your engagement on Discord, or Telegram.
- They will evaluate your attitude towards people.
- They value the size of your community that you can reach.
- They value the quality of your future referrals.

### MAIL3.ETH

### Rewards

- Exclusive goods (NFT, Cryptocurrency, Exclusive Raffle, etc.)
- Receive an Ambassador role on the Mail3 Discord server
- Receive reward from Mail3 Discord Incentive Program
- Direct access to members of the Mail3 team for questions and suggestions

### What is expected

- Write about Mail3 or produce educational content
- Produce high-quality tutorial content
- Translate Mail3 content to your language
- Take notes for Mail3 AMAs, interviews, or other online events
- Making memes & infographics
- Retweet Mail3's official announcements
- Reply to other builders' relevant tweets and mention @mail3dao
- Introduce builders to the Mail3 community
- Bring up new ideas for Mail3 related to growth, partnership, and community

### Requirements

They are currently only adding 3 to 5 new ambassadors per month to ensure they can provide each ambassador with enough support from the Mail3 Team.

- You can commit 5 hours a month in service to the community, for six months.
- At least 18 years of age
