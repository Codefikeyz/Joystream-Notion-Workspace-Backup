# Bitget - How to buy $JOY token

Tutorial how to create account on [Bitget](http://bitget.com/) and buy JOY tokens.

1. **Visit the official Bitget website at** [bitget.com](http://bitget.com/) **and click [Sign Up] on the top right corner.**
    
    ![Untitled](Bitget%20-%20How%20to%20buy%20$JOY%20token/Untitled.png)
    
2. **Choose between Email or Mobile, input your data. Set a password that adheres to the requirements, read and agree to the User Agreement and Privacy Policy, and then press [Create Account].**
    
    ![Untitled](Bitget%20-%20How%20to%20buy%20$JOY%20token/Untitled%201.png)
    
3. **Click to verify.**
    
    ![Untitled](Bitget%20-%20How%20to%20buy%20$JOY%20token/Untitled%202.png)
    
4. **Slide to complete the puzzle.**
    
    ![Untitled](Bitget%20-%20How%20to%20buy%20$JOY%20token/Untitled%203.png)
    
5. **Check your email or mobile number for a 6-digit code and copy it.**
    
    ![Untitled](Bitget%20-%20How%20to%20buy%20$JOY%20token/Untitled%204.png)
    
6. **Paste your 6-digit code and click [Sign up].**
    
    ![Untitled](Bitget%20-%20How%20to%20buy%20$JOY%20token/Untitled%205.png)
    
7. **Your account has been created. You will be redirected to Bitget main page. To access all Bitget features, please complete identity verification. Click [Verify now].**
    
    ![Untitled](Bitget%20-%20How%20to%20buy%20$JOY%20token/Untitled%206.png)
    
8. **You will be redirected to your Identity verification page, click [Verify].**
    
    ![Untitled](Bitget%20-%20How%20to%20buy%20$JOY%20token/Untitled%207.png)
    
9. **Choose between PC or Mobile verification. Use your camera on mobile to scan the QR code anc complete verification.**
    
    ![Untitled](Bitget%20-%20How%20to%20buy%20$JOY%20token/Untitled%208.png)
    
10. **Once you completed verification proceed to [dashboard](https://www.bitget.com/dashboard) and make sure your account is verified.**
    
    ![Untitled](Bitget%20-%20How%20to%20buy%20$JOY%20token/Untitled%209.png)
    
11. **It is time to improve security of your account. Click [Security].**
    
    ![Untitled](Bitget%20-%20How%20to%20buy%20$JOY%20token/Untitled%2010.png)
    
12. **Add Two-Factor Authentication to protect your account. We recommend using Google Authenticator.**
    
    ![Untitled](Bitget%20-%20How%20to%20buy%20$JOY%20token/Untitled%2011.png)
    
13. **Go to [dashboard](https://www.bitget.com/dashboard) and click [Deposit].**
    
    ![Untitled](Bitget%20-%20How%20to%20buy%20$JOY%20token/Untitled%2012.png)
    
14. **Select a crypto you wish to deposit. Make sure you have chosen the correct network and send crypto to generated address.**
    
    ![Untitled](Bitget%20-%20How%20to%20buy%20$JOY%20token/Untitled%2013.png)
    
15. **Once the transaction is confirmed by the chain and verified by Bitget, your account balance will be updated. If you have deposited another cryptocurrency, you will need to exchange it to USDT in order to purchase Joystream tokens.**
    
    ![Untitled](Bitget%20-%20How%20to%20buy%20$JOY%20token/Untitled%2014.png)
    
16. **Click [here](https://www.bitget.com/spot/JOYUSDT) or go to Markets.**
    
    ![Untitled](Bitget%20-%20How%20to%20buy%20$JOY%20token/Untitled%2015.png)
    
17. **In Markets section click [Spot], type “JOY” and click on [JOY/USDT].**
    
    ![Untitled](Bitget%20-%20How%20to%20buy%20$JOY%20token/Untitled%2016.png)
    
18. **Click [Coin overview] to make sure you are on correct page.**
    
    ![Untitled](Bitget%20-%20How%20to%20buy%20$JOY%20token/Untitled%2017.png)
    
19. **To place a BUY order, input the desired price per $JOY and the required amount. You can also input the USDT value, and the amount will be adjusted automatically. Click [Buy JOY].**
    
    ![Untitled](Bitget%20-%20How%20to%20buy%20$JOY%20token/Untitled%2018.png)
    
20. **Once your order is executed, you can check it in [Order history].**
