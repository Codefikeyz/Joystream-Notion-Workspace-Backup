## **Overview**

This document serves to identify the development path of the Joystream DAO Marketing Working Group. It includes long- and short-term planning and areas of responsibility aswell as setting WG primary goals.

## **Target Audience**

To successfully market Joystream DAO and its products, it is important to understand the target audience and what motivates them. The primary target audience for Joystream DAO is Gateway providers who are looking to boost their income and use the technical advantages of the Joystream platform in their business. Secondly, it is important to consider content creators who are looking for an alternative way to monetize their work. These content creators may be frustrated with the lack of control they have over their content on centralized platforms, or they may simply be looking for a more profitable way to make money from their work. Additionally, it is important to consider potential users of the platform who may not be familiar with blockchain technology or cryptocurrency but are interested in using the platform as a way to access quality content. Finally, it is important to create an environment that is friendly to new developers and gateway providers as they are the ones who will be able to create innovative solutions and help Joystream stay competitive in a rapidly changing industry.

## **Long Term Goals**

DAO's main goal is to provide an infrastructure and set of services, as well as an economic model, on the basis of which gateway providers can build high-quality applications.

This set of goals is specified for a period of 14-18 months.

We’ll see how things go and re-set these goals at the beginning of Q2 if necessary.

- Increase our organic traffic by 500% across all gateway providers. We expect good conversions from organic traffic so we hope to make a significant increase this year.
- Develop a gateway provider onboarding program and onboard 10 gateway providers to Joystream. Preference will be to those who already have high traffic.
- Develop a content creator onboarding program and bring 100 active content creators to Joystream. Preference will be to those who already have high traffic.
- Increase our ecosystem by bringing 50 high quality DAO contributors, and 20 developers.
- List $JOY token on tier 2 centralized exchange.
- List $JOY token on three tier 3 centralized exchanges.
- Achieve an organic growth of $JOY burnt by applications. This should be not less than 10% per month growth.
- Increase social metrics of Joystream. Increase official Twitter followers by 5000, and telegram subscriptions by 5000.

## **Marketing WG Responsibilities**

It serves as the face of Joystream, coordinating and producing all materials representing the DAO. It is the Marketing Working Group's responsibility to reach out to prospects, customers, investors, and/or the community, all while creating an overall image that positively represents Joystream DAO.

### **Branding**

- Develop a strong brand identity for the decentralized media platform DAO that resonates with target audiences and sets them apart from competitors.
- Research and analyze [joystream.org](http://joystream.org/) user experience. Propose enhancements to improve сlickthrough rate and onboarding impression.

### **Content**

Develop a content strategy that focuses on the unique features of Joystream and how it can benefit users. This should include topics such as decentralization, censorship resistance, and the ability to monetize content and features that Joystream has to offer to developers.

- Create high-quality content that is tailored to the needs of target audiences and optimized for search engine visibility.
- Develop a comprehensive content creator onboarding program: Joystream should create an onboarding program that is tailored to the needs of content creators from YouTube. This program should include resources and support for content creators to help them transition to the Joystream platform, such as tutorials, webinars, and one-on-one support.
- Coordinate with Content WG.

### **Governance**

- Coordinate with the HR working group to promote governance activities and achievements.

### **JOY token listings**

- Research potential decentralized and centralized cryptocurrency exchanges for $JOY token listing.
- Prepare necessary documentation, submit an application, monitor the process, negotiate terms, etc. Research, prepare, and present to DAO Council marketing plan to execute simultaneously with the listing.
- Research on potential launchpads. Present launchpad plan to DAO Council. The primary reason for doing a launchpad sale is that you get to tap into an existing audience, and of course, raise money. Launchpads typically take a small cut of the sales. This can bring the attention of their community without upfront investment.
- Always coordinate every listing effort with DAO Council.

### **Social Media Marketing**

- Leverage social media platforms to reach target audiences, build relationships, and drive engagement with the decentralized media platform.
- Utilize popular social media platforms such as Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, Medium, Reddit, Steemit, and Youtube and crypto communities such as forums to promote Joystream’s features and benefits. Create engaging visuals and videos that showcase the platform’s capabilities.

### **Research**

Conduct serious market research such as identifying target audiences, web3 small-time partnerships, competitors' market state, ecosystem improvements, etc.

### **Community Incentives**

Incentivizing the community will help to create a sense of ownership and engagement among users. By offering rewards for participation, users are more likely to stay engaged and be active in the Joystream DAO. Incentives can help to attract new users and encourage them to become contributors. Considering that in Joystream DAO everyone works for a salary, this point should be approached carefully, and detailed research on this topic should be done and presented to the DAO Council for approval before taking any action.

- Incentives for liquidity providers and traders.

### **Airdrops**

Airdrops are an important part of Joystream’s marketing plan because they allow the platform to reach a wider audience and increase its user base. Airdrops are a great way to spread awareness about the platform and incentivize users to join. Airdrops can help build trust with potential users by showing that the platform is committed to giving back to its community.

### **Paid Advertising**

Utilize WG budget for paid advertising channels such as Google Ads, Facebook Ads, and other relevant platforms to reach target audiences in an effective and cost-efficient manner.

### **Influencer Marketing**

- Research and identify potential influencers in the web3 space who have a large following and are interested in Joystream’s mission.
- Develop an onboarding process for new influencers that includes an introduction to the Joystream platform, a tutorial on how to use it, and a brief overview of the DAO’s governance structure.
- Create incentives for influencers for promoting DAO.

### **Public Relations**

- Develop a public relations strategy that focuses on building relationships with key stakeholders in the industry, such as journalists, bloggers, analysts, etc., to increase the visibility of the platform in relevant circles.
- Create and maintain the positive and eliminate the negative attention your project is getting.

### **Events & Conferences**

- Participate in relevant events and conferences to network with potential partners and end-users as well as increase brand awareness among industry professionals.
- Organize events such as a hackathon, community AMAs,

### **Partnerships & Affiliates**

- Identify strategic partners and affiliates who can help promote the DAO and increase its reach among potential users.

### **Customer Support & Feedback**

Ensure that customer support is available for any questions or issues related to the DAO and provide feedback mechanisms for customers to provide their input on how the DAO can be improved over time.

### **Analyze Results**

Tracking metrics such as website traffic, social media engagement, and onboarding conversions to measure the success of marketing efforts.

## **Conclusion**

Joystream DAO has an incredibly large potential for future development and scaling. More and more new opportunities are opening, and cryptocurrency tools are being adopted in the economy and marketing. There are many issues as well as marketing approaches that may be studied in the future, as this field is changing relentlessly. With every new trend, marketing strategies and tools will be customized as well, which will lead to the development of Joystream DAO's marketing strategy in general.
