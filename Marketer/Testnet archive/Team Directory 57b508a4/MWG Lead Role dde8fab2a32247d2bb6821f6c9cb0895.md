# MWG Lead Role

According to Council Term 2 lead reponsibilities [are set by Councils](https://pioneerapp.xyz/#/working-groups/openings/marketing-1) (ismail_malik, abramaria_, isonar), and they are as follows

### **Responsibilities**

- Research potential cryptocurrency exchanges for JOY token listing.
- Prepare necessary documentation: It is essential to have all of the necessary documents prepared in order to submit a successful application for a JOY token listing.
- Submit application: Once all of the required documents are ready, submission of an application can be made with exchange that has been identified as suitable during the initial research phase.
- Monitor progress: Following submission of an application, it is important to monitor progress and ensure that the listing process is moving forward as expected.
- Negotiate terms: Once an exchange has accepted a JOY token listing application, it may be necessary to negotiate terms in order to complete the listing process.
- Market launch: After all of the required steps have been completed, a market launch can be planned with announcements made on social media channels and press releases sent out to relevant publications.
- Developing strategies to increase brand visibility and recognition among the target market.
- Utilizing various channels to build awareness of the brand.
- Reach out to Web3 Founders/Developers/Potential Customers and articulate the advantages of building on Joystream.
- Respond to incoming inquiries from prospective entrepreneurs and developers that are interested in Joystream.
- Collaborate with App WG to ensure our documentation & resources for new developers is of the top notch.
