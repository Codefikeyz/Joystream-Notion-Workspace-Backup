# MWG Lead Responsibilities

These are the following responsibilities of MWG lead:

1. Follow the following roadmap in summer and winter both to drive all marketing strategies/ term plan  around it, no matter what is happening around: https://github.com/Joystream/marketing/issues/171 
2. Jsg provide the instructions which lead needs to follow each term and include progress in summary, report and make term’s plan accordingly. Here you can find instructions: [https://joystream.gitbook.io/testnet-workspace/testnet/council-period-scoring/marketers-score](https://joystream.gitbook.io/testnet-workspace/testnet/council-period-scoring/marketers-score)
3. Council asked for something sometimes, MWG is responsible to follow the council’s directions as well.
4. Because its DAO, so lead is responsible to respect sentiments of community if it doesn’t harm anything.
5. Lead is responsible to make plan for MWG each term. Plan template will be provided by council.
6. Lead is responsible to make summary of each term by MWG. Summary template will be provided by council.
7. Lead is responsible to make report of each term by MWG. Report template will be provided by council.
8. Lead is responsible to follow its made plan strictly.
9. MWG will lead its working group, take care of its workers and support them at all stages whatever their problem is regarding work.
10. MWG lead plays role of bridge between council+jsg and marketing working group.
11. MWG is responsible to bring new ideas on table and be creative in marketing strategies. MWG lead will help help jsg and council to make directions for MWG.
12. MWG lead will responsible for all marketing matters of joystream.
