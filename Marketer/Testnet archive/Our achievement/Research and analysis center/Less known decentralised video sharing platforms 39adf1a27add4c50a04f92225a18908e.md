# Less known decentralised video sharing platforms

peerTube- PeerTube is an open source, decentralized, federated video hosting service based on ActivityPub and WebTorrent technologies. Created in 2017 by a developer with the nickname Chocobozzz, further development support was taken over by the French non-profit organization Framasoft.
