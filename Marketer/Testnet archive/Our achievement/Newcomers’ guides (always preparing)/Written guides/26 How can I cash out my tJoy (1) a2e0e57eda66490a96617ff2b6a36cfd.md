# 26. How can I cash out my tJoy? (1)

Go to page [https://www.joystream.org/ru/cashout/](https://www.joystream.org/ru/cashout/)

![Untitled](26%20How%20can%20I%20cash%20out%20my%20tJoy%20(1)/Untitled.png)

Then fill up forms :

**Send from** ( need to fill up  tJOY Account address ) 

check address here  [https://dao.joystream.org/#/profile](https://dao.joystream.org/#/profile)

Click copy  Account address

![Untitled](26%20How%20can%20I%20cash%20out%20my%20tJoy%20(1)/Untitled%201.png)

and paste in field “**Send from** ”

![Untitled](26%20How%20can%20I%20cash%20out%20my%20tJoy%20(1)/Untitled%202.png)

**Amount**

Enter an amount of no more than $ 300 per transaction

![Untitled](26%20How%20can%20I%20cash%20out%20my%20tJoy%20(1)/Untitled%203.png)

**Destination address**

Enter your BCH Account address 

![Untitled](26%20How%20can%20I%20cash%20out%20my%20tJoy%20(1)/Untitled%204.png)

**Email**

Enter your email

![Untitled](26%20How%20can%20I%20cash%20out%20my%20tJoy%20(1)/Untitled%205.png)

**Joystream Handle/ID**

Enter your Handle either ID , you can check here [https://dao.joystream.org/#/profile/memberships](https://dao.joystream.org/#/profile/memberships)

![Untitled](26%20How%20can%20I%20cash%20out%20my%20tJoy%20(1)/Untitled%206.png)

**Submit**

Click submit ,but before you submit  make sure that everything is filled out correctly !You will have 10 minutes to complete the following steps

![Untitled](26%20How%20can%20I%20cash%20out%20my%20tJoy%20(1)/Untitled%207.png)

![Untitled](26%20How%20can%20I%20cash%20out%20my%20tJoy%20(1)/Untitled%208.png)

**Sending tokens**

Open new page [https://dao.joystream.org/#/profile](https://dao.joystream.org/#/profile) for sending tokens and don’t close current page [https://www.joystream.org/ru/cashout/](https://www.joystream.org/ru/cashout/)

![Untitled](26%20How%20can%20I%20cash%20out%20my%20tJoy%20(1)/Untitled%209.png)

Click send tokens 

![Untitled](26%20How%20can%20I%20cash%20out%20my%20tJoy%20(1)/Untitled%2010.png)

Click Transfer Tokens

![Untitled](26%20How%20can%20I%20cash%20out%20my%20tJoy%20(1)/Untitled%2011.png)

Click Sign Transaction and Transfer

![Untitled](26%20How%20can%20I%20cash%20out%20my%20tJoy%20(1)/Untitled%2012.png)

Enter your password and Sign the transaction

![Untitled](26%20How%20can%20I%20cash%20out%20my%20tJoy%20(1)/Untitled%2013.png)

Make sure that the transaction was successful, then return to the page [https://www.joystream.org/ru/cashout](https://www.joystream.org/ru/cashout) / and confirm **cash out**

![Untitled](26%20How%20can%20I%20cash%20out%20my%20tJoy%20(1)/Untitled%2014.png)

![Untitled](26%20How%20can%20I%20cash%20out%20my%20tJoy%20(1)/Untitled%2015.png)

After a successful notification, BCH will be sent to your wallet automatically
