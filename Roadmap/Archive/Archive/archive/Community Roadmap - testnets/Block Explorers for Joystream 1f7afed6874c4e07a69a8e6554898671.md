### **Brief Description**

*Block Explorers are essential and we do not yet have a fully functional one. As we are substrate based there are already a variety of solutions available and it would be good to explore these and understand the benefits/negatives with each one and what potential costs may be involved with utilizing them. Some customization would probably be required due to our DAO having extensive customization.*
