## Process for discussing topics within the Community Roadmap

<aside>
üëç Anyone with access to the `Community Roadmap` area on Notion can do this. People can work alone or together on this. The idea is to build up discussion and research of topics that relevant to after mainnet launch to help the community have a shared vision and goals.
None of the work has to be permanent and can be adjusted as needed.

</aside>

---

Basic

1. When basic draft is ready, a proposal is made on testnet linking to `topic` on testnet
2. Council vote to approve whether topic is worthy of community discussion
3. If approved:
    - A forum thread is made in `pre-proposal forum discussion` ([https://dao.joystream.org/#/forum/category/6](https://dao.joystream.org/#/forum/category/6))
    - The forum thread and Notion page are linked to on Discord
4. The community can provide feedback, discuss aspects and talk about ideas and introduce research
5. After some time period has elapsed, the community secretary or another person compiles the community feedback into an organized fashion on the Notion page
