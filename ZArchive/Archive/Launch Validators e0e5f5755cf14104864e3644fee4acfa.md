# Launch Validators

We invited prospective validators to share some information about themselves [here,](Meet%20Your%20Validators%20(archive)%20a4a1d8dd629d4c9fa49cdfb3e252b86d.md) so that the community had some resources to consider when deciding who to nominate.

As we move from the `frozen` phase to `thawn` on the evening of December 10th (Monday), validators can now start preparing their nodes and configuring on chain.

As many on the list doesn‚Äôt have the minimum required 41667 JOY to join the queue, Jsgenesis is offering these to provide the funding for their `stash` during these critical stages, to ensure more competition amongst the validators. We have reserved 60 accounts for this purpose, and will help as many as long as their `stash` you provided does not have enough JOY.

### How to Proceed

Sign the message below with your listed `controller` account, and send **both** the message and the signature to the email address you used to report your interest in the first place:

```jsx
I am <listed_name> with <controller>.
```

Remember of course to replace `<listed_name>` and `<controller>` with your ‚ÄúName‚Äù  and ‚ÄúController Account‚Äù from the table.

We will then bond 41667 JOY with a `stash` we control, your `controller`, and set the reward destination to your `controller` account.

### Terms

Jsgenesis reserves the right to stop this service in the event of:

- Your validator is not performing (frequently offline, warnings, high latency) over some time.
- You have not joined the **queue** within 24h of us setting up the bonded account (except during the `frozen` phase of course)
- Suspected collusion, or multiple identities

Finally, we are not planning on providing this service for longer than a month, but in any event, you will be given at least 1 weeks notice before we will unbond.
