### **Scope**

All working group period plans have the same scope:

- Current group composition.
    - Completed
- Plan for hirings (emphasizing newcomers).
    - Completed along with a suggestion for creating rules and thinking about worker rotation.
- Planned firings.
    - Completed along with a suggestion for creating rules and thinking about worker rotation.
- Onboarding plans for newcomers.
    - Completed along with plans for improving technical documentation
- Ranked list of suggestions for problems groups should attempt to tackle, and how, with corresponding budgeting, marketing or other resources needed from council or Jsgenesis.
    - Completed and includes a link to recent Distributor reliability issues from Discord which indicates the lead is following these issues and working on them.
