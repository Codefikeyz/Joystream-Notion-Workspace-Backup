# Where can I find the Joystream whitepaper?

Type: FAQ
Tags: Joystream Handbook, documents, resources
Status: Answered
Last Updated: April 30, 2022 5:14 PM

Joystream used to have a whitepaper, however it was written when the project was first launched in 2018. Since that time, many parts of the project have changed significantly, so the whitepaper is of little relevance any more and as such is no longer distributed in any form, in order to avoid confusion.

In place of the whitepaper, the best place to go is the Joystream Handbook ([https://joystream.gitbook.io/](https://joystream.gitbook.io/)). The Handbook contains high level technical information about many components of the platform.

There are no plans to update or reissue the whitepaper as it would require a significant investment of time from critical project personnel for very little benefit, when the Gitbook explains almost all technical aspects in great detail.
