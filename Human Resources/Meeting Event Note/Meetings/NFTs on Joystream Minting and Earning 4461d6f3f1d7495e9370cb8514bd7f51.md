---

**NFT Walkthrough:**

- The main focus shifts to NFTs, particularly on the Joystream platform.
- The walkthrough involves minting an NFT on Joystream using Gleev.
- Eclipsingbinary demonstrates the process by selecting videos for minting, setting royalties, and explaining the streamlined steps.
- He emphasizes the significance of royalties for creators.

---

**NFT Sale Process:**

- The walkthrough continues with the sale process of the minted NFTs.
- Eclipsingbinary demonstrates fixed price sales and auctions, explaining the options available.
- He highlights the ability to set resale royalties and briefly touch on the importance of pricing strategy, scarcity, and community engagement.

---

**Q&A and Audience Interaction:**

- Pandaprime, a participant, engages with questions about the process, such as whether one needs to be a YouTube creator to mint NFTs on Gleev.
- Eclipsingbinary clarifies that YouTube Partner Program (YPP) approval is not necessary for Joystream NFTs.
- Panda also inquires about ownership and earnings related to views, to which it is explained that the current model does not include this as a criteria for rewards.

---

**Content Creator Opportunities:**

- Eclipsingbinary expands the discussion beyond NFT sales, mentioning upcoming opportunities for content creators on Joystream.
- He touched upon the potential of launching creator tokens on Gleev.

---

**Technical Issues:**

- During the live demonstration of putting an NFT for sale, technical issues arise.
- Gleev experiences downtime, hindering the completion of the sale.
- Alternatives were explored via [L1.media](http://L1.media) to no avail.

---

**Content Reward Policy:**

- Codefikeyz steered the conversation towards NFT Reward Scheme, which involves rewarding creators for quality NFTs.
- The proposed scheme includes evaluation by content curators.
- Criteria such as quality, significance and originality, and rewards of up to 2000 $JOY per video for the top ten submissions.

---

**Conclusion:**

- The session concludes with a brief summary of the discussed topics, including the current state of Joystream, the ongoing minting activities, and the promising future for content creators on the platform.
- Eclipsingbinary expresses gratitude for the invitation and encourages participants to explore and engage in the Joystream community.
- Codefikeyz thanked the speaker and attendees for participating in the event and motivated attendees to steadily get inolved with the DAO engagement activities.
