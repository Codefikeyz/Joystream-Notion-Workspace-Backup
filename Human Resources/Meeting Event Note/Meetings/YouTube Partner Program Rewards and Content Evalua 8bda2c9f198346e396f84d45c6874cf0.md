**YPP Evaluation Channel:**

- Igrex presented the four-tier structure: Diamond, Gold, Silver, and Bronze, designed for creators of various levels.
- Detailed the rewards for each tier, including sign-up bonuses, payments per video, and referral rewards.
- Criteria for evaluating channels were discussed, emphasizing high-quality videos, professionalism, and engagement factors.
- Specific evaluation parameters included resolution, lightning, sound, script, editing, appeal, and usefulness of content.

**Live Channel Review:**

- A live review of a channel named "Crypto Star" was conducted, with Igrex finding it suitable for Bronze tier.
- Questions were raised about channels violating copyright, and Richard explained the rejection process, marking them as inappropriate.
- Igrex emphasized the importance of maintaining transparency and communicating through designated channels.

**Opting Out and Rejoining:**

- Participants inquired about creators opting out of the program and the possibility of rejoining.
- Richard clarified that creators who opt out won't be connected to Gleev, but they can sign up again in the future.
- Discussion highlighted the ongoing communication with Dmitry to address such cases.
