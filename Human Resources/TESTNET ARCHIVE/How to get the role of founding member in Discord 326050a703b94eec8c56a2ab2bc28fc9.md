# How to get the role of "founding member" in Discord

*This is only for those who are Founding Members! Please, if you are not a Founding Member, do not do this.*

### You need to specify your discord username in the pioneer membership.

1. You need to go to your membership page - [https://pioneerapp.xyz/#/profile/memberships](https://pioneerapp.xyz/#/profile/memberships)
    
    ![Untitled](How%20to%20get%20the%20role%20of%20founding%20member%20in%20Discord/Untitled.png)
    
2. Click on the pencil on the right.
    
    ![Untitled](How%20to%20get%20the%20role%20of%20founding%20member%20in%20Discord/Untitled%201.png)
    
3. Go down in the window that opens and click on the discord icon. Then specify your discord username in the field that appears. Click Save Changes.
    
    ![Untitled](How%20to%20get%20the%20role%20of%20founding%20member%20in%20Discord/Untitled%202.png)
    

### Next, go to the [#tech-support](https://discord.gg/QnpMxkmY) discord channel and tag Bedeho in the message.

Message Template:
Hey. Please give me the role of "founding member" in Discord. My pioneer membership - [https://pioneerapp.xyz/#/members/2502](https://pioneerapp.xyz/#/members/2502) 

![Untitled](How%20to%20get%20the%20role%20of%20founding%20member%20in%20Discord/Untitled%203.png)

To link to a membership, you must insert your Member ID after [https://pioneerapp.xyz/#/members/](https://pioneerapp.xyz/#/members/2502)

**Wait for the role to be given to you. This is done manually.**
