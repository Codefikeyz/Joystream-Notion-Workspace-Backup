### **From** [https://dao.joystream.org/#/bounty/preview/81](https://dao.joystream.org/#/bounty/preview/81)

**Shahzad**

[Joystream Governance App](https://dao.joystream.org/#/forum/thread/317?post=654)

If you want to join the joystream builder’s working group, this guide will help you.

Requirements:

1. Applicants are expected to have a proven track of experience in Agile, managing remote and distributed teams (for deputy lead). Please see: [https://github.com/orgs/Joystream/projects/5](https://github.com/orgs/Joystream/projects/55)5

Projects available:

1. We are looking for a deputy lead who will handle documentation or other things asked by the lead.

Rewards:

1. We create bounties for workers who are willing to join our working group - they prove their skills through a successful bounty.
2. We have a founding member program - It allows you to explore the platform and chance to become a founding member. It makes you tJOY which can cash out here right away: [https://www.joystream.org/cashout/](https://www.joystream.org/cashout/)
3. If the founding member doesn’t suit you - No problem, you can explore the platform still, stay in contact with working group leads and projects. When founding member program will end, you will start working as like you work traditionally which allows you to earn right away handsome money against your work.
4. Please watch our tutorial about founding member program: [https://www.joystream.org/start-here/what-is-joystream/](https://www.joystream.org/start-here/what-is-joystream/)
