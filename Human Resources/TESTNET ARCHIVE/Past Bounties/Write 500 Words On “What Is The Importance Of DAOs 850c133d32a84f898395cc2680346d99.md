The mechanisms underlying them are designed to allow every stakeholder to exert their say.First of all, DAO governance tokens imply the necessity for burning or spending the tokens to earn voting rights. In other words, governance tokens on DAOs introduce the ability for all users to influence the future of the DAO project.Every vote on the DAO blockchain is publicly available, meaning that the people who voted can be identified and accountability for their choice can be established. Most important of all, DAOs can prevent spamming of the community with bad ideas.Within a DAO, the voting power of each token holder is equal and they decide on how the organization is run themselves. This can be very good for organizations with an even organizational structure with no managers or leaders. No member has any additional privileges or rights other than others.
