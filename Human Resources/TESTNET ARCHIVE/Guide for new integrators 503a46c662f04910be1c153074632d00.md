## Let's start with the main tasks of the group:

- **Greeting**
Between 6am and 11pm CET.
All new persons should be greeted by a member in the HR working group in the `#welcome` or `#general` channels, offered assistance in solving CAPTCHAs if needed (#verification channel).
It's important to do it in less than 2 minutes! This affects the group's grade.
- **Onboarding**
Get new persons setup with a first membership and added to the CRM.
As soon as possible after being greeted, the objective of the HR worker is to do the following in the given order
1. Capture the person in the CRM, so that they can be catalyzed later and the project captures relevant up to date information about who is arriving, and from where,
2. Get the person to create a membership on-chain, with accurate referral information and metadata, and also some initial $tJOY to get started using the chain.
3. Get the person into a specific activity, aligned with their skills and interests, and with the needs of the network, allowing them to earn $tJOY and $JOY. This is first achieved by developing an understanding of the person in terms of skills and interests, and the filtering the set of near term opportunities through this profile. The major opportunities are
    1. Starting to work on an existing bounty, from the bounty template in the CRM, or assist them in creating a custom bounty (a new bounty specifically based on this persons skills and interests).
    2. Getting a role in a working group where there is soon space for newcomers.
    3. Running as a candidate for the council.
This onboarding should always be initiated right after the initial greeting stage, however, it may also be attempted as part of a catalyzing effort for a dormant person who never graduated into a substantive activity. The outcome of an attempt should be recorded in the CRM.
- **Catalyzing**
Get persons started with a concrete activity, such as creating a bounty, working on an existing bounty or applying for a role.
Invariably, people will drop off at various level of progression before becoming founding members. Some may never even get started beyond setting up a membership. This could be for a variety of reasons. Since we are recording all interactions in the CRM, we have visibility into the history of correspondences between the HR group and a given person, and we can see the history of activities they have been involved in. This allows the HR group to check in with people as they stall and *catalyze* them to continue their journey, as well as capture valuable information about why they stalled.
- **Nurture:**
To engage and re-engage existing persons in the CRM who are not yet founding members, to encourage them to continue, help them get answers to questions, and gather insights about their experience to support the overall success of the founding member program.

## The next part of the task is to work with the CRM system

- **Person logging**
Every new user who wrote in the #start-here channel must be added to the CRM. (You do not need to add users who have written "Hi" and requested test tokens. Only users who make contact should be added. Even if they have created a membership)
In order for you to add him to CRM, it is important that he create his membership here - [https://dao.joystream.org/#/profile/memberships](https://dao.joystream.org/#/profile/memberships) .
To find out a newcomer's membership, we put him in the #faucet channel.
- **Interaction Logging**
Every useful interaction with a user should be added to the CRM. 
The interaction must be with a result. For example: created a bounty for a user, helped create a membership, helped get into a workgroup.

## CRM guides

**The first working form is [Add New Person](https://joystream.retool.com/apps/8cf00b30-7f02-11ec-ba28-232552634a19/Joystream%20CRM%20Forms/Add%20New%20Person%20Form)**

![Untitled](Guide%20for%20new%20integrators/Untitled%202.png)

In most cases, you only need to fill out 4 lines to add a new user. But if you have other information, you need to add that too. For example: Telegram Handle and others.

**Member ID** - specify the user's membership id in the pioneer.
To do this, go to this section - [https://dao.joystream.org/#/members](https://dao.joystream.org/#/members)
Enter the user's nickname in the search and you will see the ID to the left of the user's nickname in the list.
To get the nickname, you need to carefully clarify it in the chat room. For example: Have you already created a membership? Or find the nickname in the faucet channel.

![Untitled](Guide%20for%20new%20integrators/Untitled%203.png)

**Member Handle** (Membership) - you need to specify the nickname of the user in the pioneer. How to find the nickname of the user described above.

![Untitled](Guide%20for%20new%20integrators/Untitled%204.png)

**Discovered Joystream At** - specify when the user first accessed the Joystream discord channel. 
To do this, enter the user's nickname in the discord search and find the first message from the user. View the date of the first message and enter it in the CRM.

![Untitled](Guide%20for%20new%20integrators/Untitled%205.png)

**Discord Handle** - enter the user's nickname of discord.

When you have filled in all the data you need to click on the **Create New Person** button
PLEASE DO NOT PRESS THE BUTTON MORE THAN ONCE. EVERY CLICK IS A NEW USER IN THE CRM.
