# HR Code of Conduct

It is crucial to establish clear guidelines for HR workers, who play a significant role in managing the Joystream community. Given the privileges and trust vested in these workers, it is imperative to ensure the efficient execution of their responsibilities. Therefore, the implementation of a well-defined penalty system for any misconduct in the line of duty is essential to guide and uphold the integrity of the process.

### High Level

**Communication Protocol:**

- Limit direct messages for support to essential cases; prioritize using public channels to ensure transparency and community involvement.

**Professional Integrity:**

- Prohibit the use of your role in the working group as authority in negotiations or intimidating community members such as deleting posts, kicking or banning members unjustly; maintain professional conduct at all times.

**Financial Transactions:**

- Strictly avoid involvement in any financial transactions with community members, using your role as leverage, to prevent conflicts of interest and uphold impartiality.

**Service Priority:**

- Prioritize service to the community above personal interests; ensure timely and effective support to community members.

**Confidentiality Assurance:**

- Ensure the confidentiality of DAO workers' personal information and internal HR discussions; respect privacy at all times.

**Respectful Interaction:**

- Practice respectful communication; avoid any form of discrimination or bias to create an inclusive environment for all community members.

**Adherence to Community Rules:**

- Follow the rules and guidelines of each communication platform (Discord, Telegram, etc.); avoid actions that may violate platform policies.

### Low Level

**Consistent Enforcement:**

- Apply guidelines consistently; treat all community members fairly, irrespective of their status or contributions.

**Timely Responsiveness:**

- Respond promptly to community inquiries or concerns; communicate any delays transparently and provide regular updates to the Lead.

**Documentation and Transparency:**

- Thoroughly document support interactions; maintain transparency in decision-making processes to build trust within the community.

**Continuous Training:**

- Engage in regular team training sessions to stay updated on community dynamics, tools, and protocols; commit to continuous improvement.

**Conflict Resolution:**
- Follow established processes for handling conflicts within the community or among DAO workers; prioritize resolution through open communication and mediation.

**Collaboration with Leads and Admin:**

- Collaborate with working group leads and administrators across different channels; maintain consistency in communication and moderation practices.

**Feedback Receptiveness:**

- Be open to constructive feedback from community, council members, leads and workers; use feedback as a tool for personal and professional development.

**Emergency Protocol Adherence:**

- Familiarize yourself with and adhere to protocols for handling urgent or sensitive situations; respond appropriately and efficiently in emergencies. The Lead or Discord Admin should be the first point of contact.

**Regular Self-Reflection:**

- Engage in regular self-reflection on your role and impact within the community; actively seek ways to contribute positively and also uphold a high level of professionalism in all conduct.

### Penalties

- High level guidelines: A default in this guideline will be met with
    - A 50% slash of worker’s stake in the working group
    - Eviction of the worker in the working group
    - Posible ban of the worker in the Joystream community
    
- Low level guidelines: A default in this guideline, after **previous warning**, will be met with
    - Eviction of the worker in the working group
    - 10% slash of the worker’s stake
    

The provided guidelines are subject to improvement as needed and remain valid for implementation until revised.
