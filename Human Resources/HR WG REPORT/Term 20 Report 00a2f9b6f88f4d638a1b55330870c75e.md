## Summary

This term, all tasks were executed smoothly. We've enhanced the structure of DAO daily standup meetings, collecting and implementing feedback from attendees, established boilerplate responses for FAQs, improved community support and moderation performance, recognised new hired contributors in the DAO, onboarded a new Arabic moderator who is on trial under MWG, updated the DAO workers database. We’ve also encouraged new members to update their pioneer profile, In the upcoming term, we aim to integrate all HR workers onto Telegram “community support” roles for enhanced community recognition and support efficiency. The Lead scoring test is currently in progress, and we'll share the final evaluation report upon its completion. Additionally, we've initiated activities related to Q4 KR - 1.4, 1.7, and 3.1 as outlined in the Term 21 plan. Notably, I rewarded a 10% salary bonus for two outstanding HR workers, @Genius   and @spat_sochi , for their exceptional performance this term.
