### A Continous Follow Up on Members:

For some reason or the other some individuals might not be interested to perform or get involved in the activities of the DAO, this could be due to complexity of the task, lack of resources to get involved, experience or time. A follow up is required to understand what might be the reason for the fellow not being interested and proffer solutions, issues that you do not have solutions to could be referred to or get the HR Lead involved or any other WG Lead that worker's interest may align with.

The [HR Onboarding Worksheet](Archive/How%20to%20calculate%20DAO%20workers%20salary/WIP%20Joystream%E2%80%99s%20members%202a00397eed9848e49899096ce8523c04.md) serves as a database to save Onboarding progress made, with each member and reference to have an individual fully committed into the Joystream project.

Individuals who are fully onboarded into the Joystream Project are revisited at a later time to collect data on their experiences so far, inputs and cause for improvements in the Joystream Project.
