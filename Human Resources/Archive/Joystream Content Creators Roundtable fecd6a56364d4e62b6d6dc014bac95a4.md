# Joystream Content Creators Roundtable

### Agenda:

**Introduction:**

- Introduction of the Fireside Chat concept and its purpose.
- Quick round of introductions for all participants.
- Icebreaker: A fun fact about the creator or their favorite content creation moment.
- Overview of the agenda for the session.

**Creator Spotlight:**

- Creator shares their journey as a content creator, experience, and areas of expertise.
- How they discovered Joystream.
- Their experience with other video platforms before and after joining Joystream.
- Where can people find more about the creator?

**Main Agenda (Topics for Discussion):**

1. **Creator Experience on Joystream:**
    - Sharing successes, challenges, and overall experience as a content creator on Joystream.
    - Discussing any notable achievements or milestones reached on the platform.
2. **Feedback and Suggestions:**
    - Providing feedback on the platform's usability, features, and overall user experience.
    - Offering suggestions for improvements and new features that would enhance the creator experience.
3. **Community Engagement:**
    - Exploring opportunities for creators to engage with the Joystream community and collaborate with other creators.
    - Discussing the importance of community support and networking for content creators.
4. **Content Creation Strategies:**
    - Sharing tips, strategies, and best practices for successful content creation. Tools that have revolutionised the creator's content creation process.
    - Discussing creative approaches to content creation, audience engagement, and share favourite sources of inspiration.
5. **Monetization Opportunities:**
    - Exploring current monetization opportunities available to content creators on Joystream.
    - Sharing insights on revenue generation from platforms like YouTube and other sources based on audience, engagement etc.
6. **Support and Resources:**
    - Identifying resources and support systems available to content creators on Joystream.
    - Discussing the need for additional resources or support programs tailored to creators' needs.
7. **Platform Development and Updates:**
    - Discussing recent updates and their impact on content creators.
    - Exploring upcoming features or developments that creators are anticipating.
8. **Collaboration Opportunities:**
    - Brainstorming ideas for collaboration between content creators and the Joystream team.
    - Discussing potential partnership opportunities or joint projects.
9. **Q&A Session:**
    - Open floor for creators to ask questions, seek clarification, or raise any concerns they may have.
    - Facilitating discussion and providing answers or solutions to queries raised.

**Closing Remarks:**

- Summary of key takeaways and action items from the Fireside Chat.
- Expressing gratitude to the participants for their valuable insights and contributions.
- Encouraging ongoing communication and collaboration between creators and the Joystream team.
