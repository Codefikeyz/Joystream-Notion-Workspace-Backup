# Content Creators Survey

This survey aims to gauge content creators' satisfaction and loyalty with Joystream, seeking valuable insights to calculate the Net Promoter Score (NPS). Participants provided feedback on their overall experience, platform usability, and the likelihood of recommending Joystream to others. The result will help in improving our services and better understand the needs of our community members. This report contains the data collected for the month of October and November, 2023.

**1.4 Difficulties Encountered on Gleev | L1media**

1. **Wallet Management and Password Recovery:**
    - Feedback: Users frequently lose wallets.
    - Suggestion: Enable automatic download of the JSON file to the download folder for easy account restoration in case of password loss.
2. **Sign-In Issues and Upload Challenges:**
    - Feedback: Difficulty signing in without an email, even when choosing the wallet option.
    - Upload Issues: Initial upload failures on L1media, requiring a second attempt for success.
3. **Slow Server and User Influence:**
    - Feedback: Slow performance attributed to a slow server, possibly influenced by user activity.
4. **Registration Email Case Sensitivity:**
    - Feedback: Email login issues due to case sensitivity in registration.
    - Suggestion: Advocate for case-insensitive email logins for user convenience.
5. **Website Performance and YPP Page Access:**
    - Feedback: Heavy website and occasional unavailability, particularly when accessing the YPP page.
    - Improvement Request: Server and synchronization enhancements for smoother performance and functionality.
6. **New Sign-Ups Quota and Loading Delays:**
    - Feedback: Difficulty with new sign-ups due to daily quota limits.
    - Loading Delays: Longer loading times for the "Studios" section on Gleev.
7. **Mobile Device Bugs and Display Issues:**
    - Feedback: Bugs, and display freezes when using a phone.
    - Suggestion: Address mobile device issues to ensure a smoother user experience.
8. **Dashboard Access and Navigation:**
    - Feedback: Errors and difficulties accessing the dashboard, especially on smartphones.
    - Observation: Issues with the dashboard and YouTube partner program menus potentially being on the same webpage.
9. **Account Application Challenges and Technical Support:**
    - Feedback: Some channels unable to apply; limited support response. (Rejection details)
    - Request: Improved support for channels facing application difficulties.
10. **Platform Performance and Reward Delays:**
    - Feedback: Buffering, platform non-responsiveness, and delayed reward payments.
    - Urgency: Requesting focus on content creators' reward and prompt resolution of payment delays.
11. **Referral Bonus and Payment Issues:**
    - Feedback: Missing referral bonuses and delayed payments.
    - Inquiry: Seeking clarification on payment delays and referral bonus issues.
12. **Video Tiering System and User Guidance:**
    - Feedback: Lack of clarity on the video tiering system.
    - Suggestion: Provide examples to guide new users in utilizing their Gleev accounts more effectively.

**1.5 Attempt to Mint and Sell Video NFT**

1. **Successful Attempts:**
Several creators reported successful experiences with minting and selling NFTs on Gleev || L1media, expressing their achievements with responses like "Yes," "Yes sold," and a confident "YES." A subset also noted that the process was notably easy for them.
2. **No Attempt or Lack of Knowledge:**
A notable number of respondents indicated they hadn't attempted to mint and sell videos as NFTs yet. Reasons included being new to the platform, lacking knowledge about the feature, or preferring to test blockchain and associated fees before engaging. Additionally, some mentioned discovering the feature only after reading the survey.
3. **Financial Constraints:**
Some creators mentioned financial constraints as a barrier to attempting NFT transactions. Responses included statements like "Not, I don't have money" and "Not, I didn't know I could do that," reflecting a perceived financial limitation.
4. **Exploration and Future Intentions:**
A group of creators expressed their intent to explore the NFT feature in the future. Reasons included wanting to grow their channel before attempting NFTs, being new to the platform, or planning to mint videos after gaining a more comprehensive understanding of the platform.
5. **Quality Content Consideration:**
One respondent highlighted a willingness to engage in NFTs if a dedicated program were introduced, emphasizing a preference for creating high-quality content rather than focusing on current YouTube activities.
6. **Challenges:**
A subset of creators faced challenges with their minted NFTs. Issues included limited visibility and sales, indicating potential challenges in the functionality or promotion of NFT content on the platform.

**2.0 Location or Context of Video Playback Speed Observation**

**Geographical Locations:**
The responses include users from various geographical locations such as Indonesia, Russia, Bangladesh, the United Kingdom, North America, and South America, providing a diverse range of perspectives on video playback speed.

**Feedback on Video Playback Quality:**
Users expressed generally positive feedback, using terms like "good," "ok," and "all time." Specific mentions include videos working well in terms of playback speed and buffering, contributing to a positive user experience.

**Context of Observation:**
Feedback was provided in specific contexts, including observations on Gleev, shared link feedback indicating challenges in playing videos, and instances mentioned such as watching on some videos, during video loading, and sharing in a Telegram channel.

**Playback Speed Experience:**
Users reported positive perceptions of playback speed, feeling right and similar to the original. However, concerns were raised about difficulties entering the YPP in Indonesia and the need for improvement in video playback speed for better user acquisition.

**Video Duration Preferences:**
Some users expressed preferences for video durations, specifying a minimum preference of 5 minutes to 10 minutes, indicating a desire for a certain length in the content they consume.

**Errors and Bugs During Upload:**
Concerns were raised about encountering errors and bugs during the video upload process, preventing users from providing their content, thus highlighting potential technical challenges that need attention.

**Future Intentions:**
A user expressed a definitive intention to continue creating videos, suggesting a positive outlook and commitment to the platform's video creation features.

**Additional Comments:**
Additional comments included positive acknowledgment of the overall project's performance, along with positive perceptions of fast video playback when watching on the web without buffering.
