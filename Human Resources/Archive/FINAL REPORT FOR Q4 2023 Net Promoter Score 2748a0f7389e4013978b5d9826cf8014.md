**1.4 Difficulties Encountered on Gleev | L1media**

I. **Technical Issues and User Experience:**

- Implement automatic download of the wallet's json file for easier account restoration.
- Address difficulties in signing in without email when choosing wallet.
- Resolve issues with slow uploads and the need for multiple attempts to upload.

II. **Website Performance and Server Concerns:**

- Improve website performance, as it is occasionally heavy and experiences downtime.
- Address synchronization issues, and login menu responsiveness.

III. **Registration Challenges:**

- Users encounter registration quota errors due to high demand.
- Some users experience long loading times, particularly in the "Studios" section.

IV. **Mobile Accessibility and Bugs:**

- Users report bugs and freezing displays when using phones to access their accounts.
- Difficulty opening the dashboard, especially on smartphones.

V. **Payment and Referral Issues:**

- Some users report delays in receiving payments and referral bonuses.
- Concerns about rejected memberships and discrepancies in verification decisions.

VI. **Login Process and Account Stability:**

- Users face challenges with the login process, including unexpected logouts.
- Questions about why certain users didn't progress to a higher rank.

VII. **Platform Performance and Video Viewing:**

- Complaints about buffering, slow video loading, and failed uploads.
- Users express dissatisfaction with the platform's speed and revenue delays.

VIII. **Verification and Channel Review:**

- Users question the verification team's decisions, citing disparities in requirements.
- A request for clearer examples to help users understand the video tiering system.

IX. **Language and Regional Concerns:**

- Spanish and non-English speakers report issues with website freezing and slowdowns.

**1.5 Attempt to Mint and Sell Video NFT**

The responses regarding NFT minting and sales on the platform present a mixed landscape. While some users report success in selling their NFTs, others have not yet explored this feature or are new to the platform. A subset of users has engaged in the minting process but has yet to achieve sales. In terms of awareness, there is a spectrum among users, with some being cognizant of the NFT feature but opting not to try it yet, while others lack awareness of this functionality.

Reasons for not engaging in NFT activities vary, including being new to the platform, prioritizing channel growth, and facing financial constraints. Despite these hindrances, there is a notable expression of interest among users to explore NFT minting and sales in the future. Users recognize the potential for creating high-quality content for NFTs and express a willingness to participate, particularly if a dedicated program is introduced. Some users have taken the initiative to test the NFT feature, even though challenges such as video synchronization issues and a lack of visibility or sales have been reported during their exploration.

**1.8 Improvement Suggestions for YPP v2**

Users have proposed various suggestions to enhance the Gleev platform, including the introduction of additional rewards for videos with more likes, the evaluation of channels with the awarding of bronze based on an evaluation error, and a call for openness to diverse content beyond crypto-related topics. There are also recommendations for improvements to the referral system, such as attaching an invitation letter when copying a referral link and enhancing the referral menu for better usability. Users express frustration over the inability to withdraw balances due to sudden account suspensions and highlight concerns about delayed payments, emphasizing the need for more focus on content creator revenues. Additionally, users advocate for equalizing YouTube Partner Program (YPP) rewards for all users, proposing fair payments for all videos and suggesting specific conditions for tier levels. The community also stresses the importance of server improvements to prevent overloads and advocates for listing on popular platforms like Binance or Bitkeep. Overall, the suggestions span various aspects, aiming to improve user experience, rewards systems, and platform functionality.

**2.0 Location or Context of Video Playback Speed Observation**

Users from various regions, including Indonesia, Russia, the United Kingdom, and Bangladesh, provided diverse observations about the Gleev platform. Some users reported a common feedback of videos not playing well when shared from L1 or Gleev links. However, others expressed satisfaction with the playback speed and overall performance, stating that videos work well in terms of buffering and playback speed. Users noted occasional difficulties in entering the creator program and uploading videos due to errors or bugs. While some experienced delays in loading videos, others found the web interface to be fast and without buffering issues. Feedback on video sharing was consistent, with users mentioning challenges faced by viewers in playing shared videos. Overall, users from different regions offered mixed feedback on video playback and platform performance, highlighting both positive and negative aspects of their experiences with Gleev.
