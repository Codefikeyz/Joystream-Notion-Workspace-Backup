# Joystream Roadmap Glossary

- **Ambassador:** An individual appointed to represent and promote a product, project, or organization.
- **Argus:** Joystream content delivery node [GitHub](https://github.com/Joystream/argus)
- **Atlas:** An open source video, NFT and creator token platform software kit. It is meant to be used to launch standalone new platforms which are customized to fit some particular use case. It can be customized in terms of branding, rules, types of content and moderation. [GitHub](https://github.com/Joystream/atlas)
- **Bootstrapping:** The process of starting a business or project with minimal resources or external assistance.
- **Bounties:** In the context of blockchain, rewards offered for completing specific tasks or solving particular problems.
- **Bridging:** Connecting or facilitating communication between different blockchain networks or protocols.
- **Brittle:** Fragile or easily broken, often used to describe systems or code.
- **CDN:** Content Delivery Network, a system of distributed servers that work together to deliver web content to users based on their geographic location.
- **Domain:** A distinct subset of the internet with addresses sharing a common suffix or under the control of a particular organization or individual.
- **Fiat:** a medium of exchange established as money, often by government regulation.
- **Fungible:** Interchangeable and mutually interchangeable, often used to describe assets like money or cryptocurrencies.
- **Forum:** A discussion platform on Pioneer where users can exchange ideas, information, or opinions.
- **Gleev:** Gleev is the first video platform built on top of Joystream, using the Atlas software kit. It's main purpose is to onboard the initial set of content creators to Joystream, across all content verticals.
- **Governance:** The processes and mechanisms through which decisions are made and changes are implemented within the Joystream network.
- **Hardware Wallet:** A physical device designed to securely store private keys for cryptocurrency transactions.
- **Hosting:** Providing infrastructure or services to store and make websites or applications accessible on the internet.
- **Indexing:** The process of creating an index, a statistical measure representing a group of securities.
- **Infrastructure:** The fundamental physical and organizational structures and facilities needed for the operation of a project.
- **Interoperability:** The ability of different systems or components to exchange and use information.
- **L1s:** Layer-1 chain, refers to the foundational network or main blockchain architecture. It operates as the base layer where transactions are finalized and recorded directly, examples include Bitcoin and Ethereum.
- **L2s:** Layer-2 chain extending another underlying blockchain to improve scaling or add functionality. [Learn more](https://ethereum.org/en/layer-2/)
- **Latency:** The time delay between the initiation of a process and its completion.
- **Ledger:** A hardware Crypto wallet.
- **Liquidity:** Amount of tokens in circulation on an exchange, measured in trading volume and open orders.
- **Machine Learning:** A subset of artificial intelligence that focuses on the development of algorithms enabling computers to learn from and make predictions based on data.
- **Magnitude:** The size or extent of something, often used to describe the intensity of an earthquake.
- **Metadata:** Data that provides information about other data.
- **Metamask-snap:** Plugins for the Metamask wallet [Learn more](https://metamask.io/snaps/)
- **Monetisation:** The process of earning revenue from a product, service, or platform.
- **Module:** A self-contained unit of software that performs a specific function.
- **Multisig:** Short for multi-signature, a security feature requiring multiple private keys to authorize a transaction.
- **Naive:** Lacking experience or sophistication, often used to describe simple or straightforward approaches.
- **Nomination:** Tokens staked on a validator, up to 16 nodes can be selected per nominating stash on substrate-based chains. [Learn more](https://wiki.polkadot.network/docs/glossary#active-nomination)
- **Node:** In computing, a device or point in a network where information can be sent or received.
- **Oracle:** Entity to verify someone's work product.
- **Orion:** Database with integrated query node for Atlas [GitHub](https://github.com/Joystream/orion)
- **phpBB:** A popular open-source forum software written in PHP.
- **Plugin:** An additional software component that adds specific features or functionalities to a larger software application.
- **Pro-rate:** To allocate or distribute proportionally based on a certain factor or criteria.
- **Protocol:** A set of rules or guidelines for communication between computers or devices.
- **Ramp:** A slope or incline, or may be used to describe a gradual increase or decrease.
- **SDK:** Software Development Kit, a library to support development on top of another architecture to reduce boilerplate code [Learn more](https://wiki.polkadot.network/docs/glossary#software-development-kit-sdk)
- **Software Wallet:** A digital application or program used to store and manage cryptocurrency.
- **Staking:** Participating in a proof-of-stake blockchain network by locking up a certain amount of cryptocurrency to support network operations.
- **Tangem:** Hardware wallet with closed EAL6+ chip to store crypto tokens [Learn more](https://tangem.com/)
- **Third-party:** A party other than the two principals involved in a transaction or agreement.
- **Thumbnail:** A small image that provides a preview of a larger image or video.
- **Transak:** Fiat On/Off Ramp Developer Integration for Web3 & Crypto Apps [Learn more](https://transak.com/)
- **Transcoding:** The process of converting digital data from one format to another.
- **Treasury:**
    1. On-chain budget controlled by a governing entity on substrate chains, also called council mint for Joystream.
    2. Off-chain multi-sig account to manage tokens as a group [Learn more](https://pioneer.joystreamstats.live/#/proposals/preview/497)
- **Vesting:** Scheduled unlocking of tokens [Learn more](https://handbook.joystream.org/usdjoy#release-schedule)
