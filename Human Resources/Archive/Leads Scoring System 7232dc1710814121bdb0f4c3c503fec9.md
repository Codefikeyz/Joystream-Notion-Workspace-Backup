**∆ Breakdown of Metrics:**

• Level of engagement: How interested and engaged the lead is with the organization's mission, values, and activities

• Alignment DAO's objectives

• Potential impact: The potential impact the lead can bring to the DAO in terms of network growth, marketing, or any other relevant area

• DAO experience: The lead's experience in the DAO

• Communication: The lead's communication skills and their ability to collaborate and work with a team

• Responsiveness: How promptly the lead responds to messages or enquiries from Council

• Clarity of ideas: How well the lead communicates their ideas, thoughts, and questions

• Collaborative mindset: How well the lead collaborates with other members of the team

• Participation in decision making: With regards to decentralized decision-making, the lead's willingness to contribute and participate in the DAO's decision-making process is a significant factor

• Regular meeting attendance: How often the lead attends meetings or calls

• Initiative: How often the lead takes the initiative to suggest new ideas or improvements

• Ability to deliver Commitments: The lead's reliability and ability to deliver on time and to the expected standard

• Meeting deadlines: How often the lead meets deadlines

• Quality of work: The quality of work the lead delivers

• Submission of Reports/Plans according to Template: How aligned the Lead’s report and plan is with the outlined template by the Council

• Submission of Reports/Plans within Timeframe: Adhering to the stipulated timeframe for submission of reports and plans

**∆ Single Score grading:**

1-2: Poor performance, lacking in necessary skills, not committed to the DAO's mission, and unreliable

3-4: Below average, needs improvement in one or more areas, inconsistent in their contributions, and not fully aligned with the DAO's vision.

5-6: Average, meets expectations, has some level of expertise and commitment, communicates effectively, but could improve in certain areas.

7-8: Good performance, exceeds expectations, has a high level of expertise, demonstrates commitment, communicates clearly and effectively, and aligns with the DAO's values and goals.

9-10: Excellent performance, consistently exceeds expectations, has exceptional expertise and commitment, demonstrates exceptional communication skills, and is a strong advocate for the DAO's mission and values.

**∆ Total Score Grading:**

130-140 : Excellent

120-129 : Very Good

110-119 : Good

100-109 : Satisfactory

90-99 : Fair

80-89 : Marginal

0-79 : Unsatisfactory
