# Discord Onboarding Flow

The current onboarding flow for Joystream's Discord server faces significant challenges: limited creator engagement, ineffective role assignment, vulnerabilities to malicious attacks across multiple channels, and a lack of clear member segmentation. Currently, Discord is primarily utilized by creators for support rather than community growth, with an abundance of roles allowing scammers to exploit legitimacy.

The goal is to transform Discord into a vibrant platform where creators can actively promote themselves and build their audience, token holders can stay informed and engage, potential token holders can be inspired by community activities, enthusiasts learn, network and explore exciting opportunities in Joystream. The new onboarding flow aims to streamline role assignment, enhance security measures against scams, and cultivate a well-coordinated and supportive community environment.

### **3. Perks and Engagement**

**Highlight Perks:**

- **For Creators:**
    - **Promotion in the community:** Gain visibility by showcasing your content to an engaged audience within Joystream.
    - **Opportunities for collaboration and building your audience:** Connect with other creators for joint projects and grow your follower base.
