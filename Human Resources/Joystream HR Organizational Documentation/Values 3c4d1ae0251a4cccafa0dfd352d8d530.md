# Values

- **Integrity:** The HR WG will honor it’s commitments and conduct business in a manner that promotes fairness, respect, honesty, and trust in Joysteam Community
- **Teamwork:** HR WG will support the variety of opinions, backgrounds, and experiences and recognize involvement and cooperation in all obligations.
- **Change and Innovation:** Flexibility towards creativity to support the continuous improvement of Joystream DAO through community feedback data collection
- **Advocacy:** HR will advocate for, and honor employee needs and interests whenever possible.
- **Quality Results:** HR will efficiently deliver excellent customer service and results that Joystream DAO can bank on.
