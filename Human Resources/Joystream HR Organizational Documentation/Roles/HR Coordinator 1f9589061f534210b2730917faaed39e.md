# HR Coordinator

The Coordinator plays a crucial role in the functioning and success of the HR working group, fostering documentation and addressing issues of concern within the DAO.

**RESPONSIBILITIES**

Creating and maintaining records and documents

Assisting in the implementation of projects and initiatives

Providing support to the DAO leadership

Facilitating communication and information flow

Making recommendations to the DAO’s handbook

Updating the community’s repository on Git

Evaluating the performance and effectiveness of the DAO, and implementing changes and improvements as needed to ensure the Joystream's success and sustainability.
