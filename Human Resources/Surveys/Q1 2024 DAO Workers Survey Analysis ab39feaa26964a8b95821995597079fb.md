**3.1.0 Builders Working Group - 4/7 Responses**

Summary: The feedback from workers here indicates total satisfaction with reward.

**3.1.1 Content Working Group - 6/6 Responses**

**Summary:**

Some workers expressed dissatisfaction with frequent salary reductions attributed to former council decisions and mismanaged tokenomics. They note a variable workload without corresponding salary adjustments and mentioned the absence of a fixed salary policy, leading to uncertainty despite their dedication to the project.

**Recommendation:**

Implement a transparent salary policy that considers variable workloads and tokenomics impacts. Ensure open communication channels with workers to address concerns and involve them in budget and compensation discussions. Foster transparency and fairness to maintain a motivated workforce committed to the DAO's success.

**3.1.2 Distribution Working Group - 1/7 Responses**

**Summary:**

The distribution node operator faces financial strain due to salary cuts, a drop in the $JOY rate, and higher server costs, leading to an inability to afford server expenses despite acknowledging it as coincidental.

**Recommendation:**

Reevaluate salary adjustments considering $JOY token rates and operational costs, and implement measures to alleviate financial issues on operators, ensuring network stability and sustainability.

**3.1.3 Human Resources Working Group - 2/4 Responses
Summary:**

A worker expressed uncertainty about their monthly earnings in USD due to the current HR unspecified payment structure. They emphasized the importance of clarity in income for workers and suggest fixing the payment structure in USD to facilitate understanding without disrupting the current situation.

**Recommendation:**

Address the issue of uncertainty regarding earnings by revising the HR payment structure to provide clarity in USD terms. This adjustment would enable workers to easily understand their earnings without causing disruptions to the current setup.

**3.1.4 Marketing Working Group - 2/4 Responses**

**Summary:**

Worker indicated that rewards fluctuate based on $JOY market rates and available tasks.They suggest adjusting the salary in $JOY according to the token market price for more stability and fairness.

**Recommendation:**

To ensure stability and fairness, adjust the salary in $JOY according to the token market price, aligning earnings with fluctuations in $JOY market value while maintaining consistency in compensation for marketing workers.

**3.2 Compensation Comparison**

![Screenshot_41.png](Q1%202024%20DAO%20Workers%20Survey%20Analysis/Screenshot_41.png)

Summary: Workers have varied opinions regarding the competitiveness of their compensation compared to similar roles in the industry and region. Concerns include financial risks due to fluctuating token prices affecting server expenses, unbalanced salary structures favoring council members, operating servers at a loss impacting competitiveness, and challenges in meeting expenses due to falling token prices. Some workers acknowledge non-monetary incentives and enjoyment in contributing to Joystream's success, while others compare their salaries to industry standards and express dissatisfaction with the current payment system's instability and lack of fixed policies.

Recommendation: Implement measures to mitigate financial risks associated with fluctuating token prices, ensuring stability in workers' income; reviewing and adjusting salary structures to fairly reflect performance, time and commitment amongst all workers, including council and also ensuring a fair amount of competitiveness of compensation with industry standards and regional salary levels by considering relevant factors, e.g required skills, server cost etc.

**5.0 What can be improved in the DAO?**

**Summary:**

Various improvements are suggested for the DAO, including reducing the stake of JOY for council roles to increase competition, ensuring consistency in proposal acceptance to avoid disruptions for hired workers, stabilizing the system regardless of token price fluctuations, improving marketing strategies and platform usage, ensuring fair compensation between workers and leads, increasing project visibility and attracting more creators, providing salaries in USDT for financial stability, distributing the budget more equitably among employees, promoting collaboration among workers, restoring bounty tasks for non-workers and non-investors, enhancing communication and publicity efforts, and improving interaction between working groups through goal-setting and action plans.

**Recommendation:**

1. Consider discussing concerns about the stake of JOY for council roles to foster competition and fairness in elections.
2. Stabilize the DAO salary system to provide financial stability for workers regardless of token price fluctuations.
3. Enhance marketing strategies and platform usage to increase user engagement.
4. Ensure fair compensation between workers and leads.
5. Increase project visibility and attract more creators to the platform.
6. Consider part payment of salaries in stable coin, vested payment and native token to increase motivation.
7. Increase collaboration among all DAO workers to improve efficiency and productivity.
8. Consider restoring bounty tasks to involve more community members in the DAO. (Only at Councilâ€™s full discretion if deemed neccesary).
9. Enhance interaction between working groups through goal-setting and action plans to drive development and progress within the DAO.
