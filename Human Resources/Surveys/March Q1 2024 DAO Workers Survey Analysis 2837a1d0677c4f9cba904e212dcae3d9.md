**Improvements Needed in the DAO**

Workers' suggestions for improving the DAO encompass a range of areas. Some advocate for a stronger focus on public relations to bolster visibility, while others emphasize the need to align salaries with industry standards for better retention and attraction of talent. There are also calls to reassess reward allocations, with suggestions to decrease rewards for council and validators to optimize resource utilization. On the development front, there's a consensus that investing in app and infrastructure improvements is crucial for the platform's growth and security, although this needs to be balanced with careful spending. Amidst these suggestions for enhancement, there's recognition from some quarters of the DAO's operational excellence, yet a shared sentiment prevails regarding the importance of improving communication channels and the pay structure for sustained progress.
