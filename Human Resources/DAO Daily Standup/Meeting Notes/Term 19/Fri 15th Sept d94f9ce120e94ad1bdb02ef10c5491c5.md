## Meeting Notes

### 1. Treasury signers nomination

**Discussion Points:**

- Chaos pointed out that there have been 12 candidates who applied to be a treasury wallet signer; among them, at least 7 possess a hardware wallet
- The question of the process of nomination was raised, and it was acknowledged that the council will nominate the candidates as per the written proposal [here](https://pioneerapp.xyz/#/proposals/preview/497).
- A question of whether or not being a signer is a paid role or not, it was agreed that the role came with a $100  monthly payment, but the signer could decide to waive this if they wanted to.
