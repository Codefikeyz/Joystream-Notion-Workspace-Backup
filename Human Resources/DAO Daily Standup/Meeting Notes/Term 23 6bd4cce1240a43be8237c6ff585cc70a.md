    **Meeting Note:**
    
    **Translation Service for Joystream Website**
    
    **1. Website Translation:**
    
    Discussed the languages to be included in the website translation; identified a total of 14 languages to date.
    
    - Analysis and comparison of prices and quality of services from various translation companies.
    
    **1. Budget for Translation Services:**
    
    Deliberated on the allocation of funds for website translation and how much the DAO can reasonably spend on these services.
    
    **1. Priority Languages:**
    
    Examined which languages should be given priority for translation on the website.
    
    **1. External Review:**
    
    Emphasized the importance of external review by native-speaking founding members and community members to account for terms that may not exist in certain languages.
    
    **Flat Rate Salary for All DAO Workers**
    
    **1. Salary Payment Mechanism:**
    
    The council proposed using the EMA30 conversion rate for salary payments across the DAO.
    
    **1. Stable Coin Salary Payment:**
    
    Discussion on situations in which we might need to pay salaries using stable coins from the DAO's stable coin treasury.
