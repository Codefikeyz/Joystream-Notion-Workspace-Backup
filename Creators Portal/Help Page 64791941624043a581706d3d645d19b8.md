# How to Withdraw Tokens

YPP rewards are paid to your channel account. `It is separate to your membership account`. And balance can be accessed from your channel (studio) or membership Iviewers side of Gleev) drop down

![Screenshot 2023-08-17 at 17.24.24.png](Help%20Page/Screenshot_2023-08-17_at_17.24.24.png)

Rewards  details and history are displayed in the payments dashboard in your studio view on Gleev. You can access it via this URL: **gleev.xyz/studio/payments**

Or simply go to your studio **gleev.xyz/studio**

![Screenshot 2023-08-17 at 17.21.58.png](Help%20Page/Screenshot_2023-08-17_at_17.21.58.png)

And go to payments 

![Screenshot 2023-08-17 at 17.22.28.png](Help%20Page/Screenshot_2023-08-17_at_17.22.28.png)

From the channel account JOY token can be transferred (withdrawn) to the member account via Gleev app. This can be done either from Payments Dashboard 

![Screenshot 2023-08-17 at 17.26.24.png](Help%20Page/Screenshot_2023-08-17_at_17.26.24.png)

Or from Studio Channel Profile Drop down menu

![Screenshot 2023-08-17 at 17.26.40.png](Help%20Page/Screenshot_2023-08-17_at_17.26.40.png)

Specify how much you want to withdraw and confirm the tx

![Screenshot 2023-08-17 at 17.27.55.png](Help%20Page/Screenshot_2023-08-17_at_17.27.55.png)

# My channel is suspended from YPP - what to do?

Is my channel suspended?

If your channel is suspended, the status widget in the top left corner will display the reason assigned by Joystream verification team. 

![Screenshot 2023-10-06 at 16.58.21.png](Help%20Page/Screenshot_2023-10-06_at_16.58.21.png)

Reasons for suspension

Here is the list of suspended reasons that are currently defined

`CopyrightBreach` - when YT channel reposts videos from other channels or creators. 
`MisleadingContent` - when creators are promoting content of questionable integrity, like deposit/ pyramid schemes and ml marketing. Or simply when thumbnails, channel description etc do not match with the playback content. 
`UnsupportedTopic` - Gore, violence, porn, all the categories that are in severe violation of [terms of service](https://gleev.xyz/legal/tos)
`ProgramTermsExploit` - purchased subscribers, comments and views by bots, or channels created with the aim to exploit the rewards program. For example channel has no videos or only 1 video uploaded recently. 

What can I do?

Decision about channel suspended is non-negotiable and cannot be revisited. Sync videos for suspended channels are not supported, however such channels can still benefit from the referrals scheme, which is open for all.
