## Name categories

---

- **Eponymous** (eg. Disney, Tesla, Burberry, Adidas)
- **Descriptive** (AmericanAirlines, The Home Depot)
- **Acronymic** (KFC, BP, HSBC)
- **Suggestive**
    - **Real** (Uber, Slack)
    - **Composite** (Facebook, RayBan)
    - **Invented** (Kleenex, Pinterest)
- **Associative** (‚ÄùAmazon‚Äù for wide selection, ‚ÄúSiriusXM*‚Äù* for brightest start, *‚ÄúRedBull‚Äù* for bull-like qualities such as power, confidence)
- **Non-English** (Samsung means *three stars* in Korean, Lego means play well in Danish, Zappos comes from a Spanish word *Zapatos* which means shoes, Hulu comes from Chinese proverb meaning a bowl used to store precious things)
- **Abstract** (Names such as Rolex, or Kodak have no meaning, instead rely on the power of phonetics)
