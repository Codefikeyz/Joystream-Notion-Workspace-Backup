# How to retrieve your Seed Phrase

You need your seed phrase (aka mnemonic) in order to 
1. Reset password if you forgot it 
2. Export your account to an external wallet signer ( ⚠️ *advanced users only*)

In order to retrieve your seed phrase, you need to be signed in to your account

1. Go to “My profile”  (click on icon in the top left corner)

![Screenshot 2023-08-14 at 17.12.31.png](How%20to%20retrieve%20your%20Seed%20Phrase/Screenshot_2023-08-14_at_17.12.31.png)

2. On the profile page go to settings 

![Screenshot 2023-08-14 at 17.12.40.png](How%20to%20retrieve%20your%20Seed%20Phrase/Screenshot_2023-08-14_at_17.12.40.png)

3. Go to Wallet Tab

![Screenshot 2023-08-14 at 17.12.53.png](How%20to%20retrieve%20your%20Seed%20Phrase/Screenshot_2023-08-14_at_17.12.53.png)

1. Click Export Seed  and Enter password 

2. Seed will be saved to your local machine storage
