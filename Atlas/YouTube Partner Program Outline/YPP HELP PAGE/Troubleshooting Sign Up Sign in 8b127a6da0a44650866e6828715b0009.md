# Troubleshooting Sign Up / Sign in

## Problems with sign up to YouTube Partnership Program or Signing in to your account.

**Problem:** You are unable to sign up, and Gleev app shows you the snack bar with the “Authorization Failed” error (see below).  

OR

You are unable to Sign in to your account, with a similar effect, where the error is unknown.

![Screenshot 2023-07-31 at 13.33.18.png](Troubleshooting%20Sign%20Up%20Sign%20in/Screenshot_2023-07-31_at_13.33.18.png)

## 

ℹ️ [**S](Troubleshooting%20Sign%20Up%20Sign%20in%208b127a6da0a44650866e6828715b0009.md)ome of the common things you could try:**

1. Switch off VPN
2. Clear cache and cookies
3. Try again

If it does not help, you need to report the issue using the manual below:

### **How to report the issue**

> Please do not contact members of the DAO directly asking for help.
> 

****1. Use the feedback widget to report an issue (a bug). 

![Screenshot 2023-07-31 at 13.37.16.png](Troubleshooting%20Sign%20Up%20Sign%20in/Screenshot_2023-07-31_at_13.37.16.png)

![Screenshot 2023-07-31 at 13.37.55.png](Troubleshooting%20Sign%20Up%20Sign%20in/Screenshot_2023-07-31_at_13.37.55.png)

> ⚠️ If you do not see it visible, open new browser window ***in incognito mode and accept all cookies** (as shown below)*  and you shall see the “Feedback” widget on the right hand side of the screen as shown in the screenshots above.
> 

![Screenshot 2023-07-31 at 14.32.38.png](Troubleshooting%20Sign%20Up%20Sign%20in/Screenshot_2023-07-31_at_14.32.38.png)

1. When you are reporting an issue, make sure you are attaching the screen recording:

![Screenshot 2023-07-31 at 14.39.46.png](Troubleshooting%20Sign%20Up%20Sign%20in/Screenshot_2023-07-31_at_14.39.46.png)

![Screenshot 2023-07-31 at 15.52.23.png](Troubleshooting%20Sign%20Up%20Sign%20in/Screenshot_2023-07-31_at_15.52.23.png)

1. While the screen recording is running, open the Network tab of the dev tools

Stop the recording 

4. Before sending the feedback, make sure that your video is attached, the details of your channel, membership handle and account email are provided to the development team to be able to address the issue

5. After the feedback sent, you can go to Discord support channel and post that you raised an issue, stating your `account email` or `channel ID` so we can respond to you with feedback in time asking for more details or confirming the issue is resolved.
