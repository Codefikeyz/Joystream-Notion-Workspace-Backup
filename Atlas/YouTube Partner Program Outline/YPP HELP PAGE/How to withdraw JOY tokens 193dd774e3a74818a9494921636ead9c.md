# How to withdraw JOY tokens

## How to withdraw rewards

YPP rewards are paid to your channel account. `It is separate to your membership account`. And balance can be accessed from your channel (studio) or membership Iviewers side of Gleev) drop down

![Screenshot 2023-08-17 at 17.24.24.png](How%20to%20withdraw%20JOY%20tokens/Screenshot_2023-08-17_at_17.24.24.png)

Rewards  details and history are displayed in the payments dashboard in your studio view on Gleev. You can access it via this URL: **gleev.xyz/studio/payments**

Or simply go to your studio **gleev.xyz/studio**

![Screenshot 2023-08-17 at 17.21.58.png](How%20to%20withdraw%20JOY%20tokens/Screenshot_2023-08-17_at_17.21.58.png)

And go to payments 

![Screenshot 2023-08-17 at 17.22.28.png](How%20to%20withdraw%20JOY%20tokens/Screenshot_2023-08-17_at_17.22.28.png)

From the channel account JOY token can be transferred (withdrawn) to the member account via Gleev app. This can be done either from Payments Dashboard 

![Screenshot 2023-08-17 at 17.26.24.png](How%20to%20withdraw%20JOY%20tokens/Screenshot_2023-08-17_at_17.26.24.png)

Or from Studio Channel Profile Drop down menu

![Screenshot 2023-08-17 at 17.26.40.png](How%20to%20withdraw%20JOY%20tokens/Screenshot_2023-08-17_at_17.26.40.png)

Specify how much you want to withdraw and confirm the tx

![Screenshot 2023-08-17 at 17.27.55.png](How%20to%20withdraw%20JOY%20tokens/Screenshot_2023-08-17_at_17.27.55.png)
