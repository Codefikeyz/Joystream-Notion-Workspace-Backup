**TIERS**

****In the past we have been automatically assigning tiers to the verified channels, based on the subscriber count only. Subscriber count served as benchmark for channel popularity and quality, and we used it to derive the value that such channels add to Gleev platform. 

While it served us well in distributing the rewards based on ‚Äúmerit‚Äù, it has a serious shortcoming and does not compensate all channels fairly

- Channels who purchase bots and have fake subscribers / views/ comments from ‚Äúclick farms‚Äù resulting in channels with bad content to get allocated to high tiers and get paid
- Channels with great quality but who are new and have not gained enough subscribers just yet and only growing
****

<aside>
üí° So we want to start rewarding channels based on the quality of content. Of course higher quality channels are more popular and get more views and subscribers. But sometimes it is not the case, so we have to focus on quality first, when we assign channels to the new Tiers.

**Genuine channels metrics look like this:**
Average video views in 1 month is about 10-30 % of subscribers count
1 to 3 comments per 1k views in the video
Channel posts at least once in 2-4 weeks

So if channel has 100k subscribers, they will have 10-30k views under each video, they will have more than 100 comments under each video.

</aside>

**Diamond** 
Q: Absolute gold standard of quality. Professionally done. Branded video thumbnails. Branded intro. Nice video editing, transition with special effects. Great quality of sound. Background music for reviews. Effort put in professional editing of video.  They will show themselves presenting content on full screen and mix it with product sharing.
S: Recognised influencer status. Very large subscriber audience, likely 50k+. For 500k and above that is definitely the case!
V: Average views per video ~ more than 10k per video
R: Total ratio of such channels from all channels who signup not more than 2% or less
