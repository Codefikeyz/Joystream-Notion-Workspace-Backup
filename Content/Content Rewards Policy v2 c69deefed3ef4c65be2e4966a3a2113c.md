# Content Rewards Policy v2

## Target

- Creative community support:
    
    The program stimulates the creative community on the Joystream network by providing content creators with the opportunity to receive compensation for their contributions to quality and engaging content.
    
- Improved content quality:
    
    Using evaluation criteria such as usefulness, originality and high quality helps create content that not only attracts attention, but also enriches viewers with new information and innovative approaches.
    
- Creation of positive competition:
    
    The program creates healthy competition among content creators by encouraging them to strive for excellence, experiment and provide unique types of content to cater to the diverse interests of viewers.
    
- Strengthening the Joystream community:
    
    Supporting and encouraging content creators helps strengthen the Joystream community by attracting new members and retaining existing ones through the variety and quality of content provided.
    
- Drawing attention to the platform:
    
    The program will differentiate Joystream from other platforms by incentivizing the creation of outstanding content that will attract the attention of new users and partners.
    
- Increasing brand awareness:
    
    Creating a content reward program with clear criteria and structure furthers Joystream's brand recognition as a platform that supports and rewards creativity.
    

## Main program parameters:

**Budget:** Does not exceed 1000 EMA30 USDT in Joy for each term. No more than 50 EMA30 USDT in Joy per video. No more than 1 video per channel.

**Payment schedule:** One-time payment at the end of the term.

## Content evaluation criteria:

The content evaluation system has introduced the concept of criteria by which channels apply for rewards:

- Useful videos containing new useful information that complements the general understanding of the topic;
- Unusual videos that offer an unusual or innovative approach to the topic;
- High quality video, with professional editing, high-quality image and sound. It is determined by the following parameters: resolution, fps, professional shooting (lighting, good sound, script, editing, etc.).
- The duration of the video must be justified by the topic and content.
- The channel must have an avatar, a background image and a clear description. There should be one idea (style) for each channel.
- The video must be original with a date of publication on Joystream during the term in question.

## Procedure for holding the competition

- At the beginning of Term, a Google form will be created and posted to Discord. The form will contain:
    1. Link to the video in Joystream (in Gleev)
    2. Link to video on YouTube or other media services (if available)
    3. Discord handle
    4. Proof of authorship. Required for videos uploaded outside of YPP. This could be a screen with a link to the Gleev channel mentioned in the YouTube description, backstage footage, or other proof of authorship.
- During the term, responsible curators will check and evaluate the submitted content for the previous term
- Until the end of the term Lead Curator will create Update Channel Payouts Proposal to award rewards to the channel.

## Legitimacy

To ensure the legitimacy of payments, the following will be taken into account:

- Content Licenses: You must ensure that the channel owner has all the necessary rights and permissions to use and publish the content.
- No duplication: You need to ensure that the content is not a duplicate of existing content on the platform or a crude compilation of someone else's content
- Legality of content. You must ensure that the content does not violate the laws and regulations applicable in your jurisdiction.

## Additional criteria and statistics

**Reactions and comments:** Collecting data on reactions to videos and the number of comments for cataloging and initial statistics.

**History:** Unified accounting of payments in an open Google Sheet.
