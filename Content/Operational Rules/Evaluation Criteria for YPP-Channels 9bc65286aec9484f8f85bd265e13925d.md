# Evaluation Criteria for YPP-Channels

Highly valued:

1. High-Quality Videos:
    - Ensure video resolution and frame rate align with professional standards.
    - Professional filming encompasses adequate lighting, high-quality sound, a well-crafted script, and seamless editing.
    - Video quality may also be reflected in the charisma and emotive delivery of speakers or characters.
2. Informative Videos:
    - Videos should not only provide fresh and valuable information but also enhance the audience's overall comprehension of the subject matter.
3. Unusual Videos:
    - Encourage content creators to present topics with an innovative or unconventional perspective, fostering viewer engagement through unique approaches.
4. Justified Video Length:
    - Strike a balance in video duration, avoiding both excessively short and overly long content, ensuring engagement without unnecessary verbosity.
5. Correct Channel and Metadata Formatting:
    - Channels and associated metadata must adhere to proper formatting standards, contributing to a professional and organized online presence.
6. Effort Put into Videos:
    - Channels that clearly have a lot of effort put into them are highly regarded.
7. Minimum subscribers not less than 50

Deteriorating Factors:

1. Other people's content:
    - Videos that use other people's content are less valuable, unless it is the subject of discussion or if it is due to the plot. However, unoriginal content lowers the score
2. Artificial Metrics Inflation:
    - Highlight the negative impact of attempting to artificially boost metrics such as subscribers, views, and reactions, emphasizing the importance of organic growth.
3. Clear Deception:
    - Identify and penalize any instances of objectively recognizable deception the audience.
